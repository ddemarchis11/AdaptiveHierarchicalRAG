Drosselmeyer, breaking from post 7 under Mike E. Smith and trained by Bill Mott, paid $12 for every dollar bet to win the 142nd Belmont Stakes. Fly Down, the 9-2 second choice from post 5 with John Velazquez up for Nick Zito, rallied for second. First Dude, the 7-2 favorite with Ramon Dominguez and Dale Romans, held third. Game On Dude, 10-1 under Martin Garcia for Bob Baffert, finished fourth. Stay Put, 20-1 with Jamie Theriot for Steve Margolis, crossed fifth. Interactif, also 12-1 with Javier Castellano for Todd Pletcher, came in sixth. Stately Victor, 15-1 with Alan Garcia for Mike Maker, took seventh. Ice Box, the 3-1 chalk with Jose Lezcano for Nick Zito, faded to eighth. Make Music For Me, 10-1 with Joel Rosario for Alexis Barba, ended ninth. Dave in Dixie, 20-1 with Calvin Borel for John Sadler, placed tenth. Spangled Star, longest shot at 30-1 with Garrett Gomez for Rick Dutrow, finished eleventh. Uptowncharlybrown, 10-1 with Rajiv Maragh for Kiaran McLaughlin, was disqualified from fifth and placed last for losing his eight-pound weight pad on the backstretch.
