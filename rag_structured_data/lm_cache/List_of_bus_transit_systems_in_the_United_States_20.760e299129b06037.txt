Allegany County Transit serves Cumberland in Allegany County, while Annapolis Transit operates in Anne Arundel County with Annapolis as its focal city. Cecil Transit connects Elkton within Cecil County, and Harford Transit centers on Bel Air in Harford County. Metrobus spans the Washington Metropolitan Area, anchored by Washington, D.C., whereas MTA Maryland covers the broader Baltimore-Washington Metropolitan Area with Baltimore as its primary city. Ocean City Transportation operates within Worcester County, serving the coastal city of Ocean City. RTA Central Maryland links Columbia and Laurel across Anne Arundel, Howard, and Prince George’s counties. Ride On operates solely within Montgomery County, and Shore Transit connects Salisbury across Somerset, Wicomico, and Worcester counties. TheBus serves Prince George’s County, TransIT operates in Frederick County with Frederick as its hub, and Washington County Transit centers on Hagerstown in Washington County.
