Busan IPark, based at Busan Asiad Stadium, finished 5th in the 2011 league and 6th in the playoffs. Chunnam Dragons of Gwangyang play at Gwangyang Football Stadium and came 7th. Daegu FC, at Daegu Stadium, placed 12th, while Daejeon Citizen, home at Daejeon World Cup Stadium, ended 15th. Gangwon FC, splitting home games between Gangneung Stadium and Chuncheon Stadium, propped up the table in 16th. Gwangju FC, at Gwangju World Cup Stadium, were 11th, and Gyeongnam FC of Changwon, at Changwon Football Center, were 8th. Incheon United, at Incheon Football Stadium, took 13th, and Jeju United of Seogwipo, at Jeju World Cup Stadium, were 9th. Reigning champions Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors, from Jeonju World Cup Stadium, won both the 2011 league and the playoffs. Pohang Steelers, at Pohang Steel Yard, secured 2nd in the league and 3rd in the playoffs. Sangju Sangmu Phoenix, at Sangju Civic Stadium, were 14th. Seongnam Ilhwa Chunma, at Tancheon Sports Complex, finished 10th. FC Seoul, at Seoul World Cup Stadium, were 3rd in the league and 5th in the playoffs. Suwon Samsung Bluewings, at Suwon World Cup Stadium, placed 4th in both league and playoffs. Ulsan Hyundai, at Ulsan Munsu Football Stadium, were 6th in the league and playoff runners-up.
