Phi Beta Sigma’s Alpha chapter was chartered at Howard University in Washington, DC on 15 April 1914 and remains active. Virginia State University hosts the active Alpha Alpha Alpha chapter chartered in 1931, while Lock Haven University of Pennsylvania’s Alpha Alpha Gamma, Binghamton University’s Alpha Alpha Xi, Christopher Newport University’s Alpha Alpha Pi, Johnson & Wales University’s Alpha Alpha Tau, Medgar Evers College’s Alpha Alpha Phi, Wesley College’s Alpha Alpha Psi, and Bryant University’s Alpha Beta Epsilon chapters were chartered in 1990, 1990, 1991, 6 December 1993, 30 December 1993, 6 May 1994, and an unspecified date respectively; Alpha Alpha Pi is inactive, the rest are active. Hofstra University’s Alpha Alpha Iota chapter joined in 1993 and is active. City College of New York at Harlem’s Alpha Delta chapter dates from 1929 and is active. Slippery Rock University of Pennsylvania’s Alpha Zeta Alpha was chartered 7 June 1995 and is active. West Virginia State University’s Alpha Theta, Bluefield State College’s Alpha Iota, University of Maryland Eastern Shore’s Alpha Mu (1935), Swarthmore College’s Alpha Mu Alpha, Seton Hall University’s Alpha Nu Alpha, Allen University’s Alpha Omicron (1935), Robert Morris University’s Alpha Pi Alpha (1996), and University of Pennsylvania’s Alpha Chi Alpha (1998) chapters are all active.
