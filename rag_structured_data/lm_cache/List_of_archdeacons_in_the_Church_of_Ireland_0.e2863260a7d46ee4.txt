Terry Scott has served as Archdeacon of Armagh since 2014, while Andrew Forster holds the companion post of Archdeacon of Ardboe in the same diocese from 2016. In Down and Dromore, David McClay has been Archdeacon of Down since 2013 and Roderic West Archdeacon of Dromore from the same year. Connor diocese supports three archdeacons: Stephen McBride as Archdeacon of Connor since 2002, Paul Dundas as Archdeacon of Dalriada from 2018, and George Davison as Archdeacon of Belfast since 2013. Robert Miller has been Archdeacon of Derry since 6 September 2012, with David Huss becoming Archdeacon of Raphoe in 2013. Brian John Harper was appointed Archdeacon of Clogher in 2014. Craig McCauley has held the Archdeaconry of Kilmore since 2010, and Isaac Hanna that of Elphin and Ardagh from 31 January 2016. Stephen McWhirter concurrently serves as Archdeacon of Tuam and Archdeacon of Killala and Achonry, both appointments dating from 2018. David Pierpoint has been Archdeacon of Dublin since 2004 and Neal O'Raw Archdeacon of Glendalough since 2009. Leslie Stevenson has held the united Archdeaconry of Meath and Kildare since 2009. Bob Gray became Archdeacon of Cashel, Waterford and Lismore and also Ferns in 2015, while Andrew Orr was appointed Archdeacon of Ossory and Leighlin in 2014. Adrian Wilkinson has served as Archdeacon of Cork, Cloyne and Ross since 2014.
