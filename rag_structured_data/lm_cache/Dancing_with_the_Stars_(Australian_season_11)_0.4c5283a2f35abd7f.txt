Retired cricketer Nathan Bracken and his partner Masha Belash were the first to leave on 15 May 2011, followed a week later by retired golfer Jan Stephenson and Mark Hodge. Surfer Mark Occhilupo and Jade Brand exited on 29 May, Home and Away actor Dan Ewing and Luda Kroitor on 5 June, socialite Brynne Edelsten and Arsen Kishishian on 12 June, model Nick Bracks—son of Steve Bracks—and Jessica Raffa on 19 June, model Lara Bingle and Carmelo Pizzino on 26 June, and Sunrise newsreader Samantha Armytage with Brendon Midson on 3 July. Singer-songwriter Damien Leith and Melanie Hooper claimed third place on 10 July, while model Haley Bracken—wife of Nathan Bracken—and Aric Yegudkin finished runner-up the same night. Chef and My Kitchen Rules presenter Manu Feildel and Alana Patience won the title on 10 July 2011, making Alana the first professional to win twice after her previous victory with Rob Palmer.
