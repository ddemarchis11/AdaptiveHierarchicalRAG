Ethiopia’s trio of Aselefech Mergia, Genet Getaneh and Abebu Gelan crossed first in 3:30:59, just 25 seconds ahead of Kenya’s Pamela Chepchumba, Peninah Arusei and Julia Mombi Muraga who finished in 3:31:24. Japan’s Yukiko Akaba, Miki Ohira and Yuko Machida took third place in 3:40:58. The United States foursome of Melissa White, Dorothy McMaham and Kristen Nicolini placed fourth in 3:45:06, followed by Russia’s Olesya Syreva, Lyudmila Biktasheva and Tatyana Khmeleva-Aryasova fifth in 3:48:47. Brazil’s Maria Baldaia, Marizete dos Santos and Edielza Guimarães were sixth in 3:51:11, Argentina’s Rosa Godoy, Roxana Preussler and Estela María Martínez seventh in 4:00:51, and Venezuela’s Maria Isabel Montilla, Zuleima Amaya and Mileydy Jaimes eighth in 4:00:58. Puerto Rico’s María Díaz, Zenaida Maldonado and Carmen Vallés did not finish.
