Kedah and Johor Darul Ta'zim each captured three doubles, Kedah in 1993, 2007 and 2008, Johor Darul Ta'zim in 2016, 2017 and 2019. Selangor managed the feat twice, in 1984 and again in 2009. Kuala Lumpur, Johor FA, Pahang, Singapore Lions and Kelantan each secured one double, respectively in 1988, 1991, 1992, 1994 and 2012.
