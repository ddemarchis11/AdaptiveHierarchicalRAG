Nancy Binay, running under UNA, had no prior political post but is the daughter of Vice President Jejomar Binay, former Makati mayor Elenita Binay, sister to ex-Makati mayor Jun-Jun Binay and congresswoman Abigail Binay. Tingting Cojuangco, also UNA, served Tarlac governor from 1992-1998 and is married to Congressman Jose Cojuangco Jr., cousin to Eduardo Cojuangco Jr., aunt to President Benigno Aquino III and National Youth Commission chairman Bam Aquino, sister-in-law to ex-Senator Robert Jaworski and mother-in-law to former Pasig Rep. Robert Jaworski Jr. JV Ejercito, UNA bet, has been San Juan’s lone congressman since 2010 and is President Joseph Estrada’s son, San Juan mayor Guia Gomez’s child, half-brother to Senator Jinggoy Estrada and cousin to Laguna Governor ER Ejercito. Jack Enrile, running with NPC, has held Cagayan’s 1st district House seat since 2010, is Senate President Juan Ponce Enrile’s son and husband to congresswoman Sally Ponce Enrile. Richard J. Gordon, UNA candidate, was senator 2004-2010, is married to Olongapo mayor Katherine K. Gordon and uncle to Olongapo councilor John Carlos de los Reyes. Gregorio Honasan, UNA slate member, has sat in the Senate since 2007 and lists no relatives in office. Ernesto Maceda, UNA aspirant, was senator 1971-1972 and 1987-1998 and father to Manila 4th district councilor Edward VP Maceda. Mitos Magsaysay, UNA contender, has represented Zambales’ 1st district since 2004, is daughter-in-law to Zambales governor Vic Magsaysay and niece-in-law to Senator Ramon Magsaysay Jr. Juan Miguel Zubiri, UNA bet, served senator 2007-2011, is son to Bukidnon vice-governor Jose Ma. R. Zubiri Jr. and brother to congressman Jose Zubiri III.
