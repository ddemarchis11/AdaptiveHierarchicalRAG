Alice Springs Steiner School opened in Ross in 2001, while Milkwood Steiner School began in Berrimah in 2004. Araluen Christian School, founded in Araluen in 1990, joins Gāwa Christian School on Elcho Island (2004), Māpuru Christian School in Mapuru (2010), Nhulunbuy Christian College (1998), Palmerston Christian School in Marlow Lagoon (1988), Sattler Christian College in Humpty Doo (1996) and Marrara Christian College in Karama (1979) as members of the NT Christian Schools network whose system office sits in Berrimah since 1991. Good Shepherd Lutheran College runs a primary campus in Palmerston City from 1997 and a secondary campus in Howard Springs from 2007, complemented by Living Waters Lutheran School in Larapinta (1987) and St Andrew Lutheran School in Leanyer (1983). Haileybury Rendall School in Berrimah opened in 2018, Nyangatjatjara College serves Yulara from 1997, Tiwi College operates on Melville Island, and Yipirinya School has served the Ciccone town camp since 1983. St Philip’s College, a Uniting school, has been in Alice Springs since 1965, and The Essington School Darwin, a Montessori school, opened in Nightcliff in 1990.
