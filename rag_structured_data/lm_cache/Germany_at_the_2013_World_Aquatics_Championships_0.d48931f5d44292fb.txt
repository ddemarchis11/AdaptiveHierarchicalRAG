Patrick Hausding and Sascha Klein struck gold in the men’s 10 m synchronized platform on 21 July, while Thomas Lurz, Christian Reichert and Isabelle Härle captured the open-water team title four days later. Lurz doubled his tally by winning the men’s 25 km on 27 July, having already taken silver in the 10 km on 22 July and bronze in the 5 km on 20 July. Angela Maurer matched the spectrum, earning silver in the women’s 25 km and bronze in the 10 km. Marco Koch added a swimming silver in the men’s 200 m breaststroke on 2 August, Sascha Klein claimed diving bronze in the individual 10 m platform on 28 July, and Anna Bader rounded out Germany’s medals with bronze in the women’s high diving on 30 July.
