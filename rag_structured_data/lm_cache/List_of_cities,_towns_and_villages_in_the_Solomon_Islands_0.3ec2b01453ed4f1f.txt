Honiara, the capital, counted 49,107 residents on Guadalcanal in 1999, dwarfing Auki’s 4,022 on Malaita and Gizo’s 5,323 on Ghizo Island in Western Province. Buala on Santa Isabel held 2,438 people, while fellow Isabel village Kamaosi lacked a census figure. Kirakira on Makira tallied 979 inhabitants, and tiny Maniora on the same island recorded only 100. On New Georgia, Noro registered 3,482, whereas Munda’s count was unavailable. Taro Island settlement reported 440 residents within Choiseul Province. Far southeast, Lata on Nendo Island and Tigoa on Rennell Island both lacked 1999 data, the latter lying in Rennell and Bellona Province. Off Guadalcanal’s north coast, Tulagi Island’s town counted 1,333 people under Central Province.
