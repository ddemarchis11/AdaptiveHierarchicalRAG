Brighton’s BN1 sweeps from the city centre through Coldean, Falmer, Hollingbury, Patcham, Preston, Stanmer and Withdean, straddling Brighton and Hove and Lewes, while neighbouring BN2 covers the eastern quarters—Bevendean, Brighton Marina, Kemptown, Moulsecoomb, Ovingdean, Rottingdean, Saltdean and Woodingdean—under the same two councils. Hove’s BN3 is confined to Aldrington, Hangleton and West Blatchington within Brighton and Hove. Henfield’s BN5 gathers Blackstone, Edburton, Fulking, Small Dole, Wineham and Woodmancote across Horsham and Mid Sussex. Hassocks’ BN6 threads through Albourne, Clayton, Ditchling, Goddards Green, Hurstpierpoint, Keymer, Newtimber, Sayers Common, Streat and Westmeston, shared by Lewes and Mid Sussex. Lewes itself claims BN7 with Cooksbridge, East Chiltington, Iford, Kingston, Offham, Plumpton, Plumpton Green, Rodmell, Southease, Swanborough and Telscombe, while BN8 radiates to Barcombe, Barcombe Cross, Beddingham, Chailey Green, Chiddingly, East Hoathly, Firle, Glynde, Halland, Hamsey, Holmes Hill, Laughton, Newick, North Chailey, Ringmer, Ripe, Shortgate, South Chailey, Southerham, Stoneham and Whitesmith, split between Lewes and Wealden. Newhaven’s BN9 covers Piddinghoe, South Heighton and Tarring Neville; Peacehaven’s BN10 adds Telscombe Cliffs. Worthing’s BN11 stands alone, but BN12 incorporates Ferring and Goring-by-Sea, BN13 reaches Clapham, Durrington, High Salvington, Patching and Tarring, and BN14 takes in Broadwater, Findon, Northend and Tarring, all overlapping Arun and Worthing. Lancing’s BN15 includes Coombes and Sompting within Adur and Worthing. Littlehampton’s BN16 serves Angmering, East Preston, Kingston Gorse and Rustington, while BN17 centres on Littlehampton, Climping, Lyminster and Wick, both under Arun. Arundel’s BN18 stretches from Amberley, Binsted, Burpham, Crossbush, Fontwell, Ford, Houghton, Madehurst, Poling, Slindon, Slindon Common, South Stoke, Tortington, Walberton, Warningcamp, Wepham and Yapton across Arun and Horsham. Eastbourne’s BN20 embraces Beachy Head, East Dean, Friston, Old Town, Ratton, Wannock and Willingdon with Eastbourne and Wealden; BN21 stays in the Old Town within Eastbourne, and BN22 covers Hampden Park and Willingdon shared by Eastbourne and Wealden.
