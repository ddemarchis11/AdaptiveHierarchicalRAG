On 2 June 2012 Jimmy Kamghain left Paris Saint-Germain for Kortrijk on undisclosed terms while Tail Schoonjans moved from OH Leuven to Sint-Niklaas for free. Three days later Wout Bastiaens transferred from Westerlo to OH Leuven on an undisclosed fee, Jonas Buyse and Kevin Packet both joined Wetteren on loan from Cercle Brugge, and Donovan Deekman left Lokeren for Sparta with the fee undisclosed. Ivan Lendrić arrived at Zulte Waregem from Hajduk Split and Jef Vogels went from Gent to Rupel Boom, both on undisclosed terms, on 6 June. Guy Dufour switched from Standard Liège to Sint-Truiden on 8 June, the same day that Charly, Lamisha and Tika Musonda all left Anderlecht for Chelsea in undisclosed deals, while Romain Reynaud joined Kortrijk from Châteauroux. Jesper Jørgensen’s 1.5 million-euro move from Gent to Club Brugge went through on 9 June, when Blanstel Koussalouka arrived at Kortrijk from AS Monaco and Stefan Mitrović joined the same club from Metalac Gornji Milanovac, all fees undisclosed. Nick Van Belle and Jonathan Wilmet both headed to Westerlo from Club Brugge and Mechelen respectively on 10 June, and the following day Dudu Biton left Charleroi for Standard Liège while Ludovic Buysens moved from Sint-Truiden to OH Leuven, each transfer again on undisclosed terms.
