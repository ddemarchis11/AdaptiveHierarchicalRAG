Adult Swim’s specials begin with the 2016 Adult Swim Golf Classic: Daly vs. Scott, a 72-minute match posted online after its April 8, 2016 linear debut. In 2004 Space Ghost interviewed Meatwad, Sharko and Early Cuyler for Anime Talk Show, a tongue-in-cheek rebuttal when Perfect Hair Forever bumped the Squidbillies pilot. Bagboy arrived February 20, 2015, the same night Brett Gelman’s Dinner in America premiered, followed a year later by Brett Gelman’s Dinner with Family with Brett Gelman and Family; the franchise had begun with Dinner with Friends with Brett Gelman and Friends on April 24, 2014. Michael Cusack’s 11-minute Bushworld Adventures Rick and Morty parody surprised viewers at 11 p.m. on April 1, 2018, while Maxime Simonet’s six-hour Gēmusetto Machu Picchu miniseries landed unannounced the night of March 31, 2019. Christmas in December (2009-10) mocked yuletide infomercials, Earth Ghost (April 1, 2011) replaced George Lowe and every guest with shoddy CGI clones, and Freaknik: The Musical (March 7, 2010) condensed the abandoned That Crook’d ‘Sipp series into a 60-minute T-Pain, Rick Ross and Lil Wayne special. The Greatest Event in Television History sporadically aired 2012-14, Infomercials has lampooned graveyard sales pitches since 2009, and Hunky Boys Go Ding-Dong has run since 2018. Joe Pera Helps You Find the Perfect Christmas Tree debuted December 9, 2016, KRFT Punk’s Political Party dropped March 8, 2019, and Mary Elizabeth Ellis and Artemis Pebdani’s 45-minute play adaptation Mother, May I Dance with Mary Jane’s Fist? screened at midnight January 7, 2018. Metalocalypse: The Doom Star Requiem rocked for an hour on October 27, 2013, Mr. Neighbor’s House (Brian Huskey, Jason Mantzoukas, Jesse Falcon) has spoofed Mister Rogers since 2016, and Scavengers landed December 24, 2016.
