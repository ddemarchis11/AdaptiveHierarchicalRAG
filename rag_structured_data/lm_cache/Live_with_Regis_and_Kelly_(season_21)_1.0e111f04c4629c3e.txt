On October 1, Regis Philbin and Kelly Ripa welcomed Kate Walsh, Kellie Pickler, and the Quaker Oatmeal Dance Your Heart Out segment; the next day they repeated the dance bit with Jo Frost and Diane Sawyer. October 3 saw Kelly Ripa co-hosting alongside husband Mark Consuelos, interviewing Blair Underwood, the Jon & Kate Plus 8 clan, and Robin Thicke. Regis returned October 6 with Alicia Keys, John Lithgow, and another Quaker dance segment, followed October 7 by the Toy of the Year Awards, Gary Sinise, and Sarah McLachlan. Debra Messing, Bill O'Reilly, and the Project Runway finalists appeared October 8; Molly Shannon and animal handler Peter Gros visited October 9; Russell Crowe and Isaac Mizrahi closed out the first week on October 10. Kelly Ripa paired October 13 with Randy Jackson to chat with Christian Slater and Ed Westwick, then October 14 and 15 with Anderson Cooper hosting Queen Latifah, Rusty Wallace, Nobu Matsuhisa, James Marsden, and Michael Urie. Bryant Gumbel joined Ripa October 16 for Edward Norton, Bernadette Peters, and the Project Runway winner, while Howie Mandel helped October 17 welcome Kim Raver and Billy Bob Thornton with The Boxermasters. Regis and Kelly reunited October 20 for Judge Judy, Corbin Bleu, and the CFA-Iams Cat Championship, then October 21–23 showcased Vanessa Hudgens, Jason O'Mara, Gavin Rossdale, Zac Efron, Shawn Johnson, Whoopi Goldberg, and Ashley Tisdale. Michael Chiklis co-hosted October 24 with Gisele Bündchen and John Slattery, before Regis and Kelly finished October 27–28 with Marcia Cross, Katie Brown, Charles Gibson, Tom Nardone, and Craig Horner.
