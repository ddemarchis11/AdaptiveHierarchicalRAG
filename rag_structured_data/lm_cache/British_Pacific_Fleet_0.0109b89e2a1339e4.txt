801 Naval Air Squadron flew Seafire L.IIIs from Implacable from May 1945 as part of the 8th Carrier Air Group, escorting strikes on Truk and Japan until after VJ-Day. 812 Squadron, equipped with Barracuda IIs aboard Vengeance, was at sea on VJ-Day heading for Taiwan with TG 111.2, diverted to Hong Kong, arriving 29 August. 814’s Barracuda IIs on Venerable saw no action, while 820’s Avengers on Indefatigable, after raiding Palembang and the Sakashima Gunto with 849, joined the 7th Carrier Air Group for Tokyo strikes from June 1945. 827’s Barracuda IIs on Colossus operated in the Indian Ocean from June 1945, BPF service unclear. 828 mixed Barracudas and Avengers on Implacable, 8th CAG, attacked Truk and Japan. 837’s Barracuda IIs on Glory, 16th CAG, saw no action but covered the Rabaul surrender. 848’s Avengers on Formidable hit Sakishima Gunto and Formosa, then joined 2nd CAG for July Japan strikes. 849’s Avengers on Victorious, No 2 Strike Wing, bombed Pangkalan Brandon and Palembang, then Sakashima Gunto, Formosa, and Tokyo, scoring the first bomb hit on carrier Kaiyo. 854’s Avengers on Illustrious struck Belawan Deli and Palembang, then Sakishima Gunto, joined 3rd CAG in July but saw no further action. 857’s Avengers on Indomitable raided Belawan Deli, Pangkalan Brandan, Palembang, and hammered Sakishima Gunto and Formosa, then fought suicide boats off Hong Kong on 31 August–1 September. 880’s Seafires on Implacable escorted Truk strikes, merged into 8th CAG, and attacked Japan. 885’s Hellcats on Ruler provided fleet cover, re-equipped June 1945 but saw no more action. 887’s Seafires on Indefatigable bombed Palembang, Sakashima Gunto, and Tokyo. 888’s Hellcats on Indefatigable operated over Sumatra, stayed in Ceylon when BPF sailed. 894’s Seafires on Indefatigable hit Palembang, Sakishima Gunto, and the mainland before VJ-Day. 899’s Seafire pool trained aboard Chaser and was on Arbiter at VJ-Day. 1770’s Fireflies on Indefatigable raided Palembang, Sakashima Gunto, and Formosa. 1771’s Fireflies on Implacable, 8th CAG, struck Truk in June 1945 and the mainland. 1772’s Fireflies on Indefatigable attacked the mainland until VJ-Day.
