Brian Boucher guarded the crease in 1999-2000, the same season Simon Gagne broke in as a forward. Thomas Eriksson patrolled the blue line in 1983-84, one year before Pelle Lindbergh tended goal in 1982-83. Ron Hextall followed as goaltender in 1986-87, while Eric Lindros arrived up front in 1992-93. Mikael Renberg featured as a forward in 1993-94, Chris Therien played defense in 1994-95, Janne Niinimaa in 1996-97, Joni Pitkanen in 2003-04, and Shayne Gostisbehere in 2015-16.
