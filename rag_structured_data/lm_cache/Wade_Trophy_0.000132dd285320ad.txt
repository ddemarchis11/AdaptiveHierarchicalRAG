Carol Blazejowski of Montclair State opened the Wade Trophy era as the 1977-78 senior forward. Old Dominion guard Nancy Lieberman followed with back-to-back honors, winning as a junior in 1978-79 and repeating the next year as a senior. Kansas senior guard Lynette Woodard claimed 1980-81, Louisiana Tech senior center Pam Kelly took 1981-82, and Long Beach State senior guard LaTaunya Pollard earned 1982-83. Janice Lawrence Braxton, a Louisiana Tech senior forward, won 1983-84, USC junior forward Cheryl Miller dominated 1984-85, Texas senior guard Kamie Ethridge captured 1985-86, and Villanova senior forward Shelly Pennefather closed the decade with the 1986-87 award. Louisiana Tech senior guard Teresa Weatherspoon won 1987-88, Texas senior forward Clarissa Davis followed in 1988-89, and Stanford senior guard Jennifer Azzi earned 1989-90. Tennessee senior center Daedra Charles won 1990-91, Penn State senior forward Susan Robinson took 1991-92, Nebraska senior forward Karen Jennings earned 1992-93, and Minnesota senior forward Carol Ann Shudlick claimed 1993-94. Connecticut began its trophy streak when senior center Rebecca Lobo won 1994-95, senior guard Jennifer Rizzotti repeated in 1995-96, and Florida senior forward DeLisha Milton completed the span by winning 1996-97.
