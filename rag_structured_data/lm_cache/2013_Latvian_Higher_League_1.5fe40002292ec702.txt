FC Daugava Daugavpils entered the 2013 Latvian Higher League under Ivan Tabanov, with Daniel Ola captaining the side in Erreà kits that bore no shirt sponsor. FK Daugava Rīga, managed by Virginijus Liubšys and led by Mantas Savenas, wore Nike shirts, likewise without a sponsor. Alens Vinakurovs guided Ilūkstes NSS with Dmitri Kozlov as skipper, the team also in unsponsored Erreà strips. Sergejs Golubevs’ FK Jelgava paired Deniss Petrenko’s captaincy with Macron kits sponsored by Igate. Mihails Koņevs’ FC Jūrmala relied on Alberts Nikoļskis in unsponsored Givova attire. Jānis Intenbergs’ SK Liepājas Metalurgs fielded Antons Jemeļins as captain in sponsor-free Adidas gear. Andris Riherts’ FS Metta/LU had Artūrs Biezais wearing Nike shirts carrying the SMS Credit.lv logo. Tamaz Pertia’s Skonto FC, skippered by Juris Laizāns, wore Jako kits sponsored by Granat Investment. Jurijs Popkovs led FK Spartaks Jūrmala with Dmitrijs Daņilovs in unsponsored Nike shirts. Champions FK Ventspils, managed by Jurģis Pučinsks and captained by Vitālijs Smirnovs, played the season in Adidas kits bearing the VK Tranzīts logo.
