Rickmansworth’s WD3 district blankets Rickmansworth, Chorleywood, Croxley Green, Loudwater, Mill End, Maple Cross, Sarratt, Batchworth, Chenies, West Hyde, Heronsgate and Chandler’s Cross, with spill-overs into part of Harefield, falling under Three Rivers, Chiltern and Hillingdon councils. Kings Langley’s WD4 covers Kings Langley, Chipperfield, Hunton Bridge, Rucklers Lane and Bucks Hill, split between Dacorum and Three Rivers. Abbots Langley’s WD5 serves Abbots Langley and Bedmond, straddling Three Rivers and St Albans. Borehamwood’s WD6 encompasses Borehamwood, Elstree and Well End within Hertsmere. Radlett’s WD7 includes Radlett and Shenley, shared by Hertsmere and St Albans. Watford town centre, Cassiobury and Nascot Wood lie in WD17, West Watford and Holywell in WD18, Oxhey, South Oxhey and Carpenders Park in WD19, North Watford in WD24, while WD25 stretches across Garston, Leavesden, Aldenham and Letchmore Heath, touching Watford, Hertsmere, Three Rivers and St Albans. Bushey’s WD23 covers Bushey and Bushey Heath, split between Hertsmere and Watford. A non-geographic WD99 code is reserved for the Watford Jobcentre Plus.
