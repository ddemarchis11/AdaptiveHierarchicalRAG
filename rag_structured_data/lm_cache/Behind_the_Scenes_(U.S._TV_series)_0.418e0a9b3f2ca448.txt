David Hockney demonstrates how to create the illusion of depth in drawing, with Bobby McFerrin adding musical insight, in the opening episode. Carrie Mae Weems then explores photography through the art of framing, while conductor JoAnn Falletta unpacks texture in music. Wayne Thiebaud and Matt Groening examine the power of line, and Julie Taymor reveals how to set a scene for theatre. Allen Toussaint, joined by Jane Ira Bloom and Bobby McFerrin, shows how melody works, after which painter Robert Gil de Montes delves into color. Choreographer David Parsons and B.H. Barry demonstrate pattern through dance, drummer Max Roach, MC Lyte and The Blue Man Group break down rhythm, and sculptor Nancy Graves concludes the series by explaining balance.
