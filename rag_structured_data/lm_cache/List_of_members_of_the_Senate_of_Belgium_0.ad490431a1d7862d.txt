Bert Anciaux of sp.a, Dutch-speaking, entered the Senate as a co-opted member, as did French-speaking Anne Barzin from MR, cdH’s Christophe Bastin, and Groen’s Petra De Sutter. Jan Becaus, another Dutch-language co-opted senator, represents N-VA. Geert Bourgeois, Karin Brouwers, Ann Brusseel, Sonja Claes, Cathy Coudyser, Rik Daems, Sabine de Bethune, Jean-Jacques De Gucht and Annick De Ridder all secured their seats by being chosen from within the Flemish Parliament, with party affiliations N-VA, CD & V and Open Vld. Jacques Brotchi, French-speaking MR member, was appointed by the Brussels-Capital Region’s French language group parliament, while Valérie De Bue and Alain Destexhe reached the Senate via the Parliament of the French Community. Christine Defraigne, François Desquesnes and Olivier Destrebecq, all French-speaking, were sent by the Walloon Parliament: Defraigne and Destrebecq from MR, Desquesnes from cdH.
