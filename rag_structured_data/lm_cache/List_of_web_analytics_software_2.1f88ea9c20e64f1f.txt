Adobe Systems offers Adobe Analytics version 15 and Data Workbench 6.51, both negotiating price and tracking through JavaScript cookies. Microsoft’s Bing Webmaster Tools and Google Analytics are free, with Google also selling a Premium tier at $150,000 annually. AT Internet’s Analyzer, priced by negotiation, and Bango plc’s Bango Mobile Web Analytics at $49 monthly, both rely on JavaScript cookies, though Bango adds mobile ID tracking. WizRocket’s CleverTap, Mixpanel, and SimilarWeb provide free starter tiers plus monthly plans, while LiveChat starts at $36 monthly and StatCounter ranges from free to $119 monthly. Quantcast, Mapmyuser.com, and the discontinued Yahoo! Web Analytics are completely free. Webtrekk Q3 begins at $202 monthly, Woopra scales from free to over $999 monthly, and Webtrends’ price is unlisted; all use JavaScript cookies for tracking.
