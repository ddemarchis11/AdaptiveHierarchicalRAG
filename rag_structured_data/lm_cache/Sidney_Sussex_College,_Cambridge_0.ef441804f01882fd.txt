Tony Badger, born 1947, is Paul Mellon Professor of American History at Cambridge and Master of Clare College. Rebecca Evans, born 1976, is a politician. Thomas Fuller, 1608–1661, was an author, churchman, and historian. William Du Gard, 1602–1662, was a printer. Sue Gibson, born 1960, is Research Chemist, Chair in Chemistry, and Director of the Graduate School at Imperial College London. Chris Grayling, born 1962, is Secretary of State for Transport. Dick Heckstall-Smith, 1934–2004, was a musician. John Herivel, 1918–2011, was a Bletchley Park cryptanalyst and science historian. Ronald Holmes, 1913–1981, was a colonial government official.
