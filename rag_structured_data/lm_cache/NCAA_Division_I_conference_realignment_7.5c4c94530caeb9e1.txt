Abilene Christian moved all sports except football from Division II Lone Star to Southland while its football squad became Division I FCS independent. Adelphi’s men’s soccer dropped from Division I independent status to Northeast-10 Division II. Air Force shifted men’s soccer and swimming from MPSF to WAC, and its rifle team left independent ranks for the PRC. Alaska Nanooks men’s hockey exited CCHA for WCHA and their rifle program also joined the PRC. Albany football transitioned from NEC to CAA. Azusa Pacific women’s water polo moved from CWPA to Golden Coast. Binghamton wrestling left CAA for EIWA. Boston University, except for wrestling, left America East for Patriot League, while its wrestling followed Binghamton to EIWA. Bowling Green men’s hockey followed Alaska from CCHA to WCHA. BYU softball switched from PCSC to WCC. Butler’s non-football sports advanced from Atlantic 10 to the 2013 Big East. Charleston’s entire non-football program and its swimming teams both left SoCon and CCSA respectively for CAA. Charlotte 49ers took all sports, including football starting 2015, from Atlantic 10 to Conference USA.
