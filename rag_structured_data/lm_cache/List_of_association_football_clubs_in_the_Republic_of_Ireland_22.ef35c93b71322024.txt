Bray Wanderers play at Carlisle Grounds in the seaside town of Bray, while Dundalk host matches at Oriel Park in Dundalk itself. Sporting Kilmore, based in Coolock, use Oscar Traynor Road as their home pitch, and Monaghan United occupy Gortakeegan in Monaghan. Peamount United B, from Newcastle in South Dublin, stage games at Greenogue, whereas Raheny United welcome opponents to St Anne’s Park in Raheny. St Francis call John Hyland Park in Baldonnel, Dublin, their home, and St Catherine’s take the field at Walkinstown Avenue, serving both Walkinstown and The Liberties areas of Dublin.
