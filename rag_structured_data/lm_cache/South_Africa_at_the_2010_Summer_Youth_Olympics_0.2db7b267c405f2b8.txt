Chad le Clos struck gold in the youth men’s 200 m medley on 16 August and added three silvers, finishing second in the 400 m freestyle on the 15th, the 100 m butterfly on the 16th and the 200 m butterfly on the 20th. Jacques Du Plessis claimed the boys’ discus gold on 21 August. Ruan Greyling took silver in the boys’ 400 m on the same day, while Izelle Neuhoff earned hers in the girls’ medley relay on the 23rd. Dylan Bosch secured bronze in the 200 m medley on 16 August, and together with Chad le Clos, Murray McDougall and Peter Keune he added a second bronze in the youth men’s 4 × 100 m freestyle relay that day. Samantha McIntosh contributed equestrian bronze in team jumping on 20 August, and Rudolph Pienaar leapt to bronze in the boys’ long jump on 22 August.
