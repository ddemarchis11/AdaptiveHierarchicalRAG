Cebu Eastern College, founded in 1915, fields the Dragons from its Cebu City campus as a private, non-sectarian CESAFI member. Cebu Institute of Technology–University, established 1946, sends the Wildkittens into competition under the same private, non-sectarian banner. Don Bosco Technology Center, a Salesian institution born in 1954, enters the Baby Greywolves from Cebu City. Across the Mandaue border, Sacred Heart School–Ateneo de Cebu, Jesuit since 1955, flies the Magis Eagles colors. Southwestern University PHINMA, dating to 1946, slithers in with the Baby Cobras, while the University of Cebu, founded 1964, counters with the Junior Webmasters. Its younger sibling, University of Cebu–Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue Campus, created 1995, adds the UCLM Junior Webmasters from Mandaue. The venerable University of San Carlos, tracing roots to 1595 and run by the Society of the Divine Word, charges forth as the Baby Warriors. University of San Jose-Recoletos, Augustinian Recollect since 1947, prowls as the Baby Jaguars. University of Southern Philippines Foundation, launched 1927, unleashes the Baby Panthers, and University of the Visayas, opened 1919, gallops in as the Baby Lancers.
