Joseph R. Tanner earned Eagle in 1966 and later flew on shuttle missions STS-66, STS-82, STS-97 and STS-115. Ed Tarpley, who became Eagle in 1971, served as District Attorney of Grant Parish, Louisiana from 1991 to 1997. J. L. Tarr, a 1935 Eagle, spent 43 years as a professional Scouter and became the seventh Chief Scout Executive of the BSA. Thomas L. Tatham, an Eagle from 1927, worked as an attorney and Dade County land developer while also serving as former BSA Southeast region vice president. Manti Te’o, who earned Eagle in 2008, became an All-American linebacker for the University of Notre Dame. John Tesh, a 1968 Eagle, became a New Age and contemporary Christian musician and nationally syndicated radio host. Cy Thao, a 1988 Eagle, is a Laotian-born Hmong state representative (DFL) in Minnesota. Paul Theroux, Eagle class of 1955, is a travel writer and novelist. Glenn Thompson, who earned Eagle in 1977, has represented Pennsylvania in Congress since 2009. Leo K. Thorsness, a 1948 Eagle, served as an Air Force fighter pilot, Vietnam War prisoner of war and Medal of Honor recipient. Austin Tice, Eagle 2001, is a 7th-generation Texan, Georgetown graduate, Marine Corps captain and award-winning war reporter. Rex Tillerson, a 1965 Eagle, became chairman and CEO of ExxonMobil and U.S. Secretary of State. Joseph E. Tofalo, 1977 Eagle and American Legion Eagle Scout of the Year, rose to Navy admiral and Commander of Submarine Group 10. Pat Toomey, who earned Eagle in 1977, has served as Pennsylvania’s senator since 2011. Travis Tope, a 2010 Eagle, is an actor from Texas. Alvin Townley, Eagle 1993, wrote the book Legacy of Honor. David Trick, a 1969 Eagle, is a Canadian public servant, university administrator and author. Scott Trimble, Eagle 1993, works as a location scout and manager on films including Transformers, Star Trek and Iron Man 2. Carlisle Trost, 1947 Eagle, became a Navy admiral, submariner and 23rd Chief of Naval Operations from 1996 to 2000. Richard H. Truly, a 1952 Eagle, served as Navy vice admiral, flew on shuttle missions STS-2 and STS-8 and became the first former astronaut to head NASA.
