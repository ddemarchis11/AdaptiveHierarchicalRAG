RMS Queen Elizabeth, launched in 1938, burned as Seawise University and sank in 1972, later scrapped 1974-75. MS Regal Empress, built 1953 as SS Olympia, ended as Caribe I and was scrapped in 2009. RMS Republic, launched 1903, collided with SS Florida and sank in 1909. SS Rijndam, 1951, renamed Pride of Galveston, sank en route to scrapping in 2003. SS Roma, 1926, became SS Aquila, was scuttled in 1945 and fully scrapped by 1952. SS Rotterdam, 1958, later Rembrandt, survives as a hotelship. MS Sagafjord, 1965, renamed Saga Rose, scrapped Jiangyin 2010. SS Santa Paula, 1958, served as Kuwait Marriott, bombed 1990, scrapped 2002. SS Santa Rosa, 1916, torpedoed 1942 after stints as USS Santa Rosa and SS Oregonian. SS Santa Rosa, 1932, became SS Athinai, scrapped Aliaga 1989.
