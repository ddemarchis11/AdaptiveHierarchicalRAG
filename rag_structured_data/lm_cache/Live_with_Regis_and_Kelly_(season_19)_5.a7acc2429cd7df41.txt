Regis Philbin and Kelly Ripa opened February with Sienna Miller and Katharine McPhee on the 1st, followed the next day by Jeff Probst and Charlize Theron. Chad Lowe visited on the 5th, Phil McGraw and Alex Rodriguez on the 6th, and Ashley Tisdale on the 7th. Tyler Perry and Carly Simon appeared on the 8th, and the 9th was devoted to LIVE’s Wedding 2007. Hugh Grant and Hayden Panettiere dropped by on the 12th, Drew Barrymore and Dr. Ruth on the 13th. Valentine’s week peaked on the 14th with Nicolas Cage, Katherine Heigl, a hidden-camera lingerie exposé and last-minute gift ideas. Eva Mendes, children’s group Hi-5 and kids discussing love filled the 15th, while Martin Lawrence, Gabrielle Union and Daniel G. Amen guested on the 16th. Beautiful Baby Week began on the 19th alongside Marisa Tomei and bubble scientist Fan Yang, continued on the 20th with Heather Graham and the fresh Daytona 500 winner, welcomed William H. Macy on the 21st, John Travolta and Richard Roeper on the 22nd, and on the 23rd featured Billy Bob Thornton, Jesse L. Martin, Geraldo Rivera and toy expert Chris Byrne. The show traveled to Los Angeles on the 26th for Tim Allen, Masi Oka and Joan & Melissa Rivers’ Oscar recap, stayed for Courteney Cox, David Boreanaz and Five for Fighting on the 27th while Regis revisited favorite L.A. spots, and wrapped the month on the 28th with Emily Procter, Ryan Seacrest and Regis touring the Deal or No Deal set.
