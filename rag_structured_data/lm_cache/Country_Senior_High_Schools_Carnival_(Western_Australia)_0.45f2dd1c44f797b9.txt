In 2005 Bunbury Senior High School won the boys basketball title while Geraldton Senior College claimed both the boys football and boys hockey crowns, and Albany Senior High School took out the boys soccer championship. Geraldton Senior College repeated as basketball victors in 2006, the same year Mandurah Senior College won football, Bunbury Cathedral Grammar School won hockey, and Newton Moore Senior High School won soccer. Australind Senior High School secured both basketball and soccer honours in 2007, with Newton Moore Senior High School winning football and Bunbury Cathedral Grammar School retaining the hockey title. Eastern Goldfields College won the 2008 basketball competition, Pinjarra Senior High School won football, Great Southern Grammar won hockey, and Mandurah Senior College won soccer. Bunbury Senior High School regained the basketball title in 2009, Mandurah Senior College repeated as football champions, Great Southern Grammar kept the hockey crown, and Australind Senior High School again won soccer. Bunbury Senior High School won basketball once more in 2010, Albany Senior High School won football, Great Southern Grammar continued its hockey dominance, and Bunbury Cathedral Grammar School won soccer. Denmark High School won the 2011 basketball title, Albany Senior High School won football, Bunbury Cathedral Grammar School won hockey, and Mandurah Senior College won soccer. Bunbury Senior High School won basketball again in 2012, Albany Senior High School won both football and soccer, and Narrogin Senior High School won hockey. Bunbury Senior High School achieved a triple in 2013, winning basketball, hockey, and soccer, while Newton Moore Senior High School won football. Geraldton Senior College won basketball in 2014, Great Southern Grammar won football, Narrogin Senior High School won hockey, and Albany Senior High School won soccer. Australind Senior High School won basketball in 2015, Newton Moore Senior High School won football, Narrogin Senior High School retained hockey, and Bunbury Cathedral Grammar School won soccer. Busselton Senior High School won the 2016 basketball title, Newton Moore Senior High School won football again, Great Southern Grammar won hockey, and Albany Senior High School won soccer.
