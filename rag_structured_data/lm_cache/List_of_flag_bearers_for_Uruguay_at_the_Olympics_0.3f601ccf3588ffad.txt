Dolores Moreira carried Uruguay’s flag in the 2016 Summer Games as a sailor, following rower Rodolfo Collazo who did so in 2012, Alejandro Foglia in 2008, and Ricardo Fabini in 1992. Swimmer Serrana Fernández held the honor in 2004, preceded by athlete Mónica Falcioni in 2000, tennis player Marcelo Filippini in 1996, and rower Jesús Posse in 1988. Basketball player Carlos Peinado was flag bearer in 1984, while Gabriel Hottegindre represented Uruguay at the 1998 Winter Olympics in skiing. Earlier, Darwin Piñeyrúa led the delegation in 1972 as an athlete, Ana María did so in 1968 as a swimmer, Rubén Etchebarne in 1964 as a cyclist, and Luis M. Aguiar in 1960 as a rower. Héctor Costa carried the flag in 1956 as a basketball player, Estrella Puente in 1952 as an athlete, and Guillermo Douglas inaugurated the sequence in 1932 as a rower.
