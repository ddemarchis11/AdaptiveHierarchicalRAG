The Annis Mound and Village Site, listed 21 December 1985, occupies the western bank of the Green River in Horseshoe Bend northeast of Logansport. The Baby Track Rock Petroglyphs, added 8 September 1989 with location restricted, lie near Morgantown. Carlston Annis Shell Mound, recorded 1 April 1986, sits on the east side of the Green River off Kentucky Route 403 outside Schulztown. Morgantown’s 205 S. Main St. holds the John Carson House, enrolled 26 July 1991, while the Carson-Annis Ferry Farm at 1086 Annis Ferry Road joined the Register 31 July 1998. One block north of US 231 and KY 403 stands the Confederate-Union Veterans’ Monument, listed 17 July 1997. DeWeese Shell Mound, added 1 April 1986, crowns the eastern riverbank in Horseshoe Bend west of Taylor Lake above Highview. Woodbury’s junction of KY 403 and Hime Street hosts the Finney Hotel, registered 7 November 1995; the same town’s Woodbury Park contains the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Superintendent’s House and Workmen’s Office, listed 19 June 1980. Beside US 231, the Ice House on Little Muddy Creek entered the Register 8 January 1987. Rayburn Johnson Shell Mound, Read Shell Mound, Russell Shell Mound and Woodbury Shell Midden all joined on 1 April 1986, occupying respective river bends near Prentiss, Monticello, Logansport and Woodbury. Reedyville Petroglyphs along Lock 5 Road north of the Green River and Turkey Rock Petroglyphs near Morgantown, both with restricted details, were listed 8 September 1989.
