Galina Lazarova and Marian Kolev topped the 2004 Aerobic Gymnastics World Championships with 19.780 points for Bulgaria. Spain’s Alba de las Heras and Jonatan Canada followed just 0.030 behind at 19.750, while Romania’s Izabela Lăcătuș and Remus Nicolai claimed third with 19.700. Italy’s Giovanna Lecis and Wilkie Satti Sanchez placed fourth on 19.250, and South Korea’s In Yung Choi and Jong Kun Song were fifth with 18.800. Russia’s Elena Kopylova and Aleksander Shulga earned 18.530 for sixth, Brazil’s Juliana Antero and Marcisnei Oliveira scored 18.400 for seventh, and Chile’s Carolina Chacon Molinez and J.P. Olivares Arancibia closed the rankings at 18.050.
