The Methodist Church of Alberton, the Methodist Episcopal Church of Anaconda, its counterpart in Bozeman, the Methodist Episcopal Church and Parsonage in Bridger, the Fromberg Methodist-Episcopal Church, the First Methodist Episcopal Church and Parsonage in Glendive, the First United Methodist Church Parsonage and the Union Bethel African Methodist Episcopal Church—both in Great Falls—the Scandinavian Methodist Church in Kalispell, the Methodist-Episcopal Church of Marysville, the Stevensville Grade School-United Methodist Church, the Methodist Episcopal Church in Third Forks, and the Stateler Memorial Methodist Church in Willow Creek are all NRHP-listed Montana landmarks. The First Episcopal Methodist Church of Chester, erected in 1911, gained NRHP recognition in 1997, as did the 1894-built Methodist Episcopal Church South in Corvallis, honored the same year.
