Red Star Belgrade topped the 2018-19 Serbian SuperLiga, the same season they first entered the top flight in 1946-47, and they play at Red Star Stadium with site crvenazvezdafk.com. Runners-up Radnički Niš date their debut season back to 1935-36, returned to the elite in 2012-13, and host matches at Stadion Čair under fkradnickinis.rs. Third-placed Partizan Belgrade also trace their continuous top-tier presence to 1946-47 and use Partizan Stadium via partizan.rs. Čukarički finished fourth, originally reached the top division in 1995-96, regained promotion in 2013-14, and occupy Čukarički Stadium with fkcukaricki.rs. Fifth-placed Mladost Lučani share the same 1995-96 first-season mark, came back up in 2014-15, and play at Mladost Stadium on fkmladostlucani.com. Napredak, sixth, first appeared in 1951, re-entered in 2016-17, and share Mladost Stadium through fknapredak.rs. Vojvodina placed seventh, debuted in 1931-32, last promoted for 1987-88, and use Karađorđe Stadium at fkvojvodina.rs. Proleter were eighth in their maiden top-flight campaign 2018-19, play at Karađorđe Stadium, and publish at fkproleternovisad.rs. Radnik Surdulica, ninth, entered in 2015-16 and stay at City Stadium via fk-radnik.com. Spartak Subotica, tenth, first played in 1946-47, last promoted in 2009-10, and host at Subotica City Stadium on fkspartak.com. Voždovac, 11th, debuted in 2005-06, returned in 2013-14, and use Voždovac Stadium at fkvozdovac.rs. Mačva, 12th, date back to 1930-31, came up in 2017-18, and play at Gradski stadion Šabac under fkmacva.com. Rad Belgrade, 13th, first reached the top in 1987-88, regained status in 2008-09, and occupy Stadion Kralj Petar I via fkrad.rs. Promoted sides entering in 2019-20 include First-League winners TSC Bačka Topola, who will debut at Bačka Topola City Stadium on fktsc.com; First-League runners-up Javor-Matis, back after first arriving in 2002-03 and using Ivanjica Stadium at fkjavor.com; and First-League third-placers Inđija, returning after their 2010-11 debut season and hosting at Inđija Stadium via fkindjija.com.
