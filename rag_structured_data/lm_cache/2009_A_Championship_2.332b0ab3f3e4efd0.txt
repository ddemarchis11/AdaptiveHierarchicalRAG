Bohemians A played out of Phibsborough at the AUL Complex, while Bray Wanderers A were based in Bray and used Jackson Park. F.C. Carlow, hailing from Ballon, hosted matches at The Valley, and Cobh Ramblers, rooted in Cobh, occupied St Colman’s Park. Cork City A represented Cork at Turner’s Cross, and Sporting Fingal A operated from Fingal with home games at the ALSAA Sports Complex. St Patrick’s Athletic A, from Inchicore, played at Richmond Park, whereas champions Shamrock Rovers A, stationed in Tallaght, staged their fixtures at Tallaght Stadium. Tralee Dynamos, finally, called Tralee home and lined up at Cahermoneen.
