Darren Lockyer, captain of the Brisbane Broncos and Queensland, wore Australian cap 661, while vice-captain Cameron Smith, also of Melbourne Storm, held 738. Queenslanders dominated the squad: North Queensland Cowboys Johnathan Thurston (724) and Willie Tonga (723), South Sydney’s Greg Inglis (737), Melbourne Storm trio Billy Slater (751), Cooper Cronk (744) and Cameron Smith, plus Brisbane’s Sam Thaiday (739) and the uncapped Corey Parker and Jharal Yow Yeh. New South Wales supplied Penrith’s Luke Lewis (715), Cronulla’s Paul Gallen (750), Manly pair Anthony Watmaugh (758) and Tony Williams, Wests Tigers hooker Robbie Farah (764), Canterbury centre Josh Morris (767), Canberra prop David Shillington (765) and Newcastle flyer Akuila Uate. Darius Boyd (761) crossed from St George Illawarra, while Manly’s Daly Cherry-Evans, yet to earn a cap, completed the 2011 Four Nations roster.
