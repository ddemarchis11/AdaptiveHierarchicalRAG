Aquimisa Carbajosa plays out of the Municipal gym in Carbajosa de la Sagrada, Castile and León, while Ávila Auténtica Carrefour El Bulevar hosts rivals at the Carlos Sastre arena in Ávila and Basket León uses León’s Palacio de los Deportes. Caja Rural de Zamora performs in the Ángel Nieto sports hall in Zamora and Universidad de Valladolid competes at Fuente de la Mora in Valladolid, keeping the Castile and León contingent at five clubs. Galicia supplies six teams: Calvo Basket Xiria from Carballo’s Vila de Noia, Estudiantes Lugo Leyma Natura at Lugo’s Pazo dos Deportes, KFC Culle in Culleredo’s O Burgo, Obradoiro B Silleda at Santiago’s Fontes do Sar, Porriño Baloncesto Base in the Porriño 2 pavilion, Santo Domingo Betanzos in Betanzos’ Municipal hall, and Ucoga Seguros CB Chantada in Chantada’s Municipal gym. Gijón Basket 2015 represents Asturias in the Palacio de Deportes of Gijón, and USAL La Antigua completes the group, playing at the Würzburg arena in Salamanca, Castile and León.
