Aksys Games brought Arc System Works’ Guilty Gear XX Accent Core Plus to PlayStation Portable on April 7, 2009, followed on February 25, 2010 by BlazBlue: Calamity Trigger Portable, and on May 31, 2011 by BlazBlue: Continuum Shift II, all for PSP. March 25, 2010 saw the PSP debut of extreme’s shooter Cho Aniki Zero via PSN, while Kogado Studio and Premium Agency’s Mimana Iyar Chronicle reached PSP on March 30, 2010. Goshow’s Gladiator Begins launched September 14, 2010, Neverland’s tactical RPG Blazing Souls Accelate arrived October 19, 2010, and Opus Studio’s Jikandia: The Timeless Land released March 15, 2011, each exclusively on PSP. Type-Moon’s Fate/Extra materialized November 1, 2011, Idea Factory and Otomate’s otome title Hakuoki: Demon of the Fleeting Blossom debuted February 14, 2012, and its spin-off Hakuoki: Warriors of the Shinsengumi followed November 6, 2012, all on PSP. GungHo, Apollosoft and Chime’s Ragnarok Tactics landed February 19, 2013, while Idea Factory, Otomate and Comcept’s puzzle visual-novel hybrid Sweet Fuse: At Your Side closed the PSP slate on August 27, 2013.
