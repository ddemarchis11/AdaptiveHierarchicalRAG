Ganganagar and Bikaner are the first two Lok Sabha seats, both reserved for Scheduled Castes, while Churu, Jhunjhunu, Sikar, Jaipur Rural, Jaipur, and Alwar follow as unreserved constituencies numbered 3 through 8. Bharatpur and Karauli-Dholpur, at positions 9 and 10, revert to SC reservation. Dausa at 11 is the first Scheduled Tribe seat, succeeded by the unreserved Tonk-Sawai Madhopur, Ajmer, Nagaur, Pali, Jodhpur, Barmer, and Jalore up to number 18. Udaipur and Banswara close the list as ST-reserved constituencies 19 and 20.
