North Macedonia’s first Olympic flag bearer was wrestler Shaban Tërstena at the 1996 Summer Games. Alpine skier Jana Nikolovska carried the flag twice, in the 1998 Winter Olympics and again in 2002. Basketball player Blagoja Georgievski led the delegation at the 2004 Summer Games, followed by alpine skier Gjorgji Markovski in 2006. Canoeist Atanas Nikolovski had the honor in 2008, and alpine skier Antonio Ristevski in 2010. Swimmer Marko Blaževski was the flag bearer for the 2012 Summer Olympics, while cross-country skier Darko Damjanovski did so in 2014. Swimmer Anastasia Bogdanovski carried the flag at the 2016 Summer Games, and cross-country skier Stavre Jada in the 2018 Winter Olympics.
