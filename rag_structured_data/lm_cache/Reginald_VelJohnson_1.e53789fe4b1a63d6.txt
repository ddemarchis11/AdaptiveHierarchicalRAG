Reginald VelJohnson first appeared on television in 1985 as Harmon Hunter in The Equalizer episode “Lady Cop,” returned to the series in 1986 as Arthur Williams for “Dead Drop,” and again in 1988 for “Sea of Fire.” In 1988 he played The Santa Thief in the 227 Christmas episode “The Night They Arrested Santa Claus,” then introduced Carl Winslow on Perfect Strangers in the 1989 episode “Crimebusters,” a role he carried through 215 episodes of Family Matters from 1989 to 1998. He voiced Dad in the 1994 television movie A Cool Like That Christmas, portrayed Det. Bob Hymes in the 1994 television movie One of Her Own, and appeared as Ed Conroy in the 1996 television movie Deadly Pursuits. VelJohnson checked in as a Hotel Guest for the 1992 Tales from the Crypt episode “Werewolf Concerto,” suited up as Santa for the 1993 Dream On episode “Silent Night, Holy Cow,” and played Dr. David Sinclair in the 1998 Diagnosis: Murder episode “The Last Resort.” From 1999 onward he turned up as Rev. Bennett on The Hughleys episode “Daddy’s Going to Hell,” Dr. David Bryant on Twice in a Lifetime episode “Whistle Blower,” Maurice Beaumont on The Fugitive episode “Lagniappe,” Dr. Phillip Kane across two 2002 CSI: Crime Scene Investigation episodes, Mr. Holden on Crossing Jordan episode “Someone to Count On,” Dr. Kaplan on Will & Grace episode “It’s the Gay Pumpkin, Charlie Brown,” Sterling Oglevee on The Parkers episode “A Sterling Relationship,” Dikembe the Ambassador on That’s So Raven episode “The Royal Treatment,” Danny Small on Ghost Whisperer episode “Undead Comic,” and Rev. Everett on Eve episode “Oh, Brother.”
