Herb Brooks was honored in 2002 after serving the Devils during the 1992-1993 season, while Neal Broten received recognition in 1998 following his 1995-1996 tenure. Mike Emrick, honored in 2004, had two separate stints with the team from 1982-1986 and again from 1993-2011. Phil Housley was honored in 2008 after his single 1996 season, whereas Mark Johnson received his honor in 2011 for his service from 1985-1990. Lou Lamoriello was recognized in 1992 during his long tenure from 1987-2015, Max McNab was honored in 1998 after serving from 1983-1987, and Lou Vairo received his honor in 2000 following his 1984-1986 service with the organization.
