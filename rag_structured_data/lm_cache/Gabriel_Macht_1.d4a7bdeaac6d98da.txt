Gabriel Macht first appeared on television in 1991 as Tal Weaver in the Beverly Hills, 90210 episode “Leading from the Heart.” He followed with the 1995 TV movie Follow the River, portraying Johnny Draper, and later dropped by Spin City in 1997 as The Naked Guy in the “Snowbound” episode. In 1998 he played Barkley, a model-obsessed artist, in Sex and the City’s “Models and Mortals,” then took the role of Luke in the 1999 Wasteland installment “Best Laid Plans.” Macht embodied William Holden in the 2000 TV movie The Audrey Hepburn Story that same year he began a 13-episode run as Dr. Mark Gabriel, the main character of the short-lived series The Others. After an unaired 2005 Numb3rs pilot appearance as Don Eppes, he surfaced in the Archangel miniseries as R.J. O’Brian. From 2011 through 2019 Macht starred as Harvey Specter across nine seasons and 134 episodes of Suits, also serving as producer, and briefly reprised the role in a 2019 Pearson episode.
