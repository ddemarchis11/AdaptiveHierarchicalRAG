On 26 June 2013, Triad transferred six Savannah-area stations to the future Alpha Media group: WRWN 107.9 MHz in Port Royal, WGCO 98.3 MHz in Midway, WFXH-FM 106.1 MHz on Hilton Head Island, WUBB 106.9 MHz in Bluffton, WHHW 1130 kHz on Hilton Head Island, and W241CV 93.5 MHz, also on Hilton Head Island. Tama Broadcasting added WXYY 100.1 MHz in Rincon on 3 March 2014. The same day, YMF also transferred six Jackson, MS outlets previously acquired on 24 September 2013: WJNT 1180 kHz in Pearl, WOAD 1300 kHz, WJQS 1400 kHz, WJMI 99.7 MHz, WRKS 105.9 MHz in Pickens, and WKXI-FM 107.5 MHz in Magee.
