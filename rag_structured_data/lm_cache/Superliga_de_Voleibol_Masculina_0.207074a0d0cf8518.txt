CV Teruel plays at Los Planos in Teruel, a 3,500-seat arena. Unicaja Almería hosts matches at Moisés Ruiz, which holds 1,750 spectators in Almería. Ushuaïa Ibiza Voley fills Es Viver, a 1,000-capacity venue on Ibiza. Urbia Voley Palma occupies Son Moix, the largest hall listed, with 5,076 seats in Palma. UBE L'Illa-Grau uses Ciutat Esportiva, a 700-seat facility in Castellón de la Plana. Textil Santanderina competes at Matilde de la Torre, a 1,000-seat gym in Cabezón de la Sal. Vecindario ACE Gran Canaria gathers 3,000 fans at Pabellón Municipal in Vecindario, Santa Lucía de Tirajana. Conectabalear CV Manacor plays in the intimate 500-seat Na Capellera in Manacor. Río Duero-Soria takes the court at Los Pajaritos, a 2,000-seat arena in Soria. Melilla stages home games at Javier Imbroda Ortiz, a 3,800-seat venue in the autonomous city of Melilla. FC Barcelona occupies Parc Esportiu Llobregat, a 2,500-seat complex in Barcelona. Intasa San Sadurniño closes the list at Pavillón Municipal, the smallest venue with 300 seats in San Sadurniño.
