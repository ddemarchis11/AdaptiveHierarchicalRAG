Ekaterina Stroynova, Ekaterina Loginova and Gouzel Khassanova topped the 2004 World Sports Acrobatics Championships in Liévin with 22.420 points for Russia. Jessica Stamenovic, Tara Bushbridge and Veronica Gravolin took second place for Australia on 19.670, just 0.040 ahead of Danielle Heider, Samantha Schabow and Jennifer Da Silva who claimed third for the United States with 19.630. Fourth place went to Olexandra Gorkovenko, Alla Basiuk and Larisa Semeniuk of Ukraine on 19.210, while another Russian squad—Elena Kirilova, Elena Moiseeva and Tatiana Alexeeva—ranked fifth with 18.990. Kazakhstan’s Gaukhar Ahmetova, Alexandra Yenina and Aigul Dukenbayeva scored 18.910 for sixth, Great Britain’s Yvonne Weish, Stefanie Akroyd and Victoria Pattison posted 18.810 for seventh, and Germany’s Sophie Schwassmann, Susanne Schaeffer and Natalia Arent closed the top eight on 17.610.
