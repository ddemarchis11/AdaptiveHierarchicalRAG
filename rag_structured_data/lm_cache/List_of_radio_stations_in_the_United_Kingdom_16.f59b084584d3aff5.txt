Chelmsford Calling served Chelmsford on 104.4 FM until it closed in February 2007, while Hackney’s Sound Radio broadcast on 1503 AM until June 2007. TGR Sound signed off Bexleyheath’s 103.7 FM on 22 June 2008, and Enniskillen’s Vibe FM left 105.3 FM in October 2009. Forest of Dean Radio, which had reached Cinderford on 1521 AM and Newent on 1503 AM, fell silent on 31 December 2009. Link FM’s 92.2 FM for Harold Hill ended on 28 June 2010, and Belfast’s Féile FM closed 103.2 FM on 25 March 2011. Boundary Sound’s 102.6 FM for Newark shut down on 23 June 2011, the same summer that Voice Radio’s DAB 11C service for Cardiff and Newport ceased on 20 July 2011 and Wayland Radio’s 107.3 FM for Wayland, Norfolk, ended on 27 August 2011. Burngreave Community Radio’s 103.1 FM for Burngreave, Sheffield, closed in September 2011, followed by Radio Sandwell’s 106.9 FM for Sandwell, West Midlands, in November 2011. XS—formerly Afan FM—left Neath on 97.4 FM and Port Talbot on 107.9 FM on 13 December 2011. Cheshire FM closed Mid Cheshire’s 92.5 FM on 27 January 2012, Rossendale Radio signed off 104.7 FM for Haslingden and Rawtenstall on 5 March 2012 (returning in December 2018), Diversity FM ended Lancaster’s 103.5 FM on 31 March 2012, and The Park closed Lymington’s 97.9 FM on 8 June 2012. ON FM left Hammersmith, London, on 101.4 FM in January 2013, OX105 FM shut Oxford’s 105.1 FM on 2 October 2013, and Stroud FM ended Stroud’s 107.9 FM on 12 February 2014.
