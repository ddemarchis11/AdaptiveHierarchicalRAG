Atlanta Blaze, founded in 2016, plays field lacrosse at Fifth Third Bank Stadium in Major League Lacrosse and has yet to win a title. The Atlanta Gladiators, an ECHL ice-hockey side established in 2003, skate at Infinite Energy Arena without a championship. Atlanta Reign, born in 2018, competes in the Overwatch League at Coca-Cola Roxy and remains title-less. Atlanta United 2, a 2018 USL Championship soccer club, also uses Fifth Third Bank Stadium and has no silverware. The College Park Skyhawks, a 2019 NBA G League basketball outfit, host games at Gateway Center Arena and have not yet claimed a title. Georgia Swarm, launched in 2015, plays box lacrosse at Infinite Energy Arena and captured the National Lacrosse League crown in 2017. Gwinnett Stripers, a 2009 International League (AAA) baseball team, play at Coolray Field without a championship. Rugby ATL, founded in 2020, fields a rugby-union side at Lupo Family Field in Major League Rugby and is still seeking its first title.
