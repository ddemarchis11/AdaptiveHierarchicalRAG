In 2014, striker Ahmad Qushairi Mustafa and forward Ahmad Azriddin Rosli both joined Felda United F.C. without a transfer fee, while fellow striker Azahari Radzali moved to Perlis FA under the same free terms. Midfielders Tunku Noor Hidayat Tunku Ishak, Mohd Amirul Hisyam Awang Kechik, Muhd Azri Zulkiflee, Mohd Helmi Hariri Ibrahim, and Muhd Farhan Hazmi Mohd Nasir respectively switched to Penang FA, Harimau Muda A, Kuala Lumpur FA, Kuala Lumpur FA again, and Kuala Lumpur FA once more, all for zero fee. Defender Gusti Ishak Fitri Shah Said also arrived at Kuala Lumpur FA gratis, as did Muhd Faizzwan Dorahim at Perak FA. Utility man Muhd Syazwan Zaipol Bahari, attacking midfielder Muhammad Irfan Zakaria, and defender Mohd Faris Shah Rosli all signed for Harimau Muda B without cost. Central midfielder Muhd Azalinullah Mohd Alias and centre-back Mohd Arif Fadzilah Abu Bakar both transferred to T-Team F.C. for free, goalkeeper Abdul Gafur Samsudin went to Selangor FA on a free, and defender Muhd Azrul Zulkifli joined Sungai Ara F.C. without a fee. Finally, right-sided attacker Muhd Shahrul Hakim Rahim and attacking midfielder Muhd Syahrizal Shaharin both headed to Negeri Sembilan FA for nothing, while central midfielder Shakir Zufayri Ibrahim completed a cost-free move to Terengganu FA.
