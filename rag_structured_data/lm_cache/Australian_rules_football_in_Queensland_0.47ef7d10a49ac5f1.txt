Jason Akermanis, raised in Brisbane, debuted in 1996 and kicked 421 goals in 325 games, winning a Brownlow and a place in Queensland’s Team of the Century after representing the Australian Football Alliance. David Armitage came from Morningside to debut in 2007 and has 150 games and 89 goals, while Marcus Ashcroft, the Gold-Coast-raised Southport product who first pulled on AFL boots in 1989, became the first Queenslander to 300 games, finished with 318 and 145 goals, and claimed three Brisbane Lions premierships plus Team of the Century honours. Claye Beams, Labrador-raised, joined the elite in 2011 for 30 games and 21 goals, and his Southport-raised brother Dayne Beams followed in 2009, amassing 112 games, 118 goals, a 2010 Collingwood flag and 2012 All-Australian selection. Clint Bizzell, born and bred in Brisbane via Wilston Grange, debuted in 1996 for 163 games and 79 goals, and Mount Isa product Simon Black, 1998 debutant, has 320 games, 170 goals and a Brownlow. Maryborough-born Jamie Charman, from Sandgate Hawks, started in 2001 for 129 games and 55 goals and represented the 2008 AFL Dream Team, while Cairns-raised Che Cockatoo-Collins, originally from Brisbane, kicked 215 goals in 160 games after debuting in 1994. Robert Copeland, Northern Eagles graduate, debuted in 2001 for 143 games and 39 goals, and Brisbane-born Gavin Crosisca, from Western Districts, played 246 games and 64 goals from 1987, represented Queensland and sits in the Team of the Century. Morningside pair Courtenay Dempsey (2006, 133 games, 35 goals) and Scott Harding (2006, 50 games, 15 goals) followed, with Cairns-raised Charlie Dixon (Redland, 2011, 49 games, 53 goals), Northern Eagles product Josh Drummond (2005, 94 games, 35 goals), Brisbane legend Jason Dunstall (Coorparoo, 1985, 269 games, 1254 goals, four State of Origin appearances, Allies representative, Hall of Famer and Team of the Century), Queensland-raised Mitch Hahn (Zillmere, 2000, 181 games, 164 goals), dual-Hawthorn-premiership Gold-Coaster David Hale (Broadbeach, 2003, 237 games, 217 goals), Cairns-born Jarrod Harbrow (2007, 120 games, 34 goals) and Charleville’s Rhan Hooper (Ipswich Eagles, 2006, 54 games, 56 goals) completing the Queensland-born AFL roll-call.
