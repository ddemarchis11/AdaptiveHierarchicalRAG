Disney’s The Lion King led 1994’s global box office with $763,455,561, while Paramount’s Forrest Gump followed at $677,945,399. 20th Century Fox placed third with True Lies at $378,882,411 and fifth with Speed at $350,448,145. Warner Bros claimed two spots: The Mask earned $351,583,407 in fourth place, and Dumb and Dumber took seventh with $247,275,374, while Interview with the Vampire nabbed ninth at $223,664,608. Universal’s The Flintstones landed sixth with $341,631,208, Gramercy’s Four Weddings and a Funeral eighth at $245,700,832, and Paramount rounded out the top ten as Clear and Present Danger collected $215,887,717.
