Sharreth first sat on the Asianet panel for Idea Star Singer 2007, returned for the 2008 edition, stayed through season 4 spanning 2009-2010, and closed the stint with season 5 in 2010-2011. In 2009 he crossed to Zee Tamil to judge Sa Re Ga Ma Pa 2009 Challenge. Kairali TV enlisted him for Gandharva Sangeetham in 2012, Amrita TV brought him aboard Super star the ultimate in 2013 and kept him for Super Star in 2014, later recalling him for Super Star Junior in 2016. Mazhavil Manorama engaged him twice in 2013—Josco Indian voice season 2 and Candyman Indian Voice Junior—then welcomed him back for Super 4 in 2018 and, from 2019 to the present, for Padaam namuk Paadam. Flowers TV added him to Bhima Indian Music League in 2015.
