Malacca FA entered the 2012 Malaysia FAM League led by coach G. Selvamohan, with Amrie Abdullah captaining the side in Ambros kits bearing Metaforce branding. Edres Selamat guided Melodi Jaya SC, whose captain Mohd Fairuz Abdul Rahman wore Nike shirts sponsored by PJM. Zabidi Hassan coached Penang Water Supply Board, where Mat Saiful Mohamad skippered Line7 kits emblazoned with PBAPP. Jánós Krécská directed Penang FA, whose captain Hasmawi Hassan donned Adidas gear carrying Allianz University College of Medical Science across the chest. Tajuddin Nor oversaw KP Shahzan Muda FC, captained by Raja Mohd Hafillah Raja Mamat in Kappa apparel without a shirt sponsor. Halim led Kepala Batas FC, with Safuan Azwan Abdul Shukor as captain wearing Lancast kits. Mohd Nizam Jamil coached KL SPA FC, where Sazali Suwandi captained the team in Joma shirts sponsored by Suruhanjaya Perkidmatan Awam. Abdul Kamal commanded Tentera Darat, with Josep Amar as captain in adidas kits featuring Honda sponsorship. Azman Eusoff guided UiTM FC, whose captain Ahmad Shahir Ismail wore Umbro shirts carrying the UiTM name.
