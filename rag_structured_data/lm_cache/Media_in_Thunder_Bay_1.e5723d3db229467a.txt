CBC Radio One blankets Northwestern Ontario on FM 88.3 as CBQT-FM, while its French-language sibling Ici Radio-Canada Première occupies 89.3 CBON-FM-20, relaying Sudbury’s signal. Thunder Bay Information Radio Inc. runs 90.5 CKSI-FM, a tourist and emergency channel, and Superior Info Radio’s 97.1 CITB-FM likewise serves visitors. Dougall Media operates two locals: 91.5 CKPR-FM, adult contemporary since migrating from AM 580, and Rock 94 CJSD-FM at 94.3. St. Joseph’s Care Group’s CJOA 95.1 brings Christian music, once echoed at Candy Mountain 93.1 until 2005. First Nations and Christian programming reach the dial via 98.1 CJTL-FM-1, rebroadcasting Pickle Lake’s CJTL-FM. Acadia Broadcasting owns 99.9 The Bay CJUK-FM for classic hits and 105.3 Country 105 CKTG-FM, which carried CJLB call letters until 2005. Lakehead University students air campus voices on 102.7 LU Radio CILU-FM, and CBC Music closes the roster at 101.7 CBQ-FM.
