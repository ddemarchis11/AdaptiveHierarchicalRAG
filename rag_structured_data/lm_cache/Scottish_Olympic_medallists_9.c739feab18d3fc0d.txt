Andy Murray struck gold for Scotland in the men’s singles tennis, while Callum Skinner topped the podium twice, first in the track-cycling men’s team sprint and later with silver in the individual sprint. On the rowing lake Heather Stanning captured gold in the women’s coxless pair and Katie Archibald did likewise in the women’s team pursuit on the track. Four Scots—Daniel Wallace, Duncan Scott, Robert Renwick and Stephen Milne—shared silver in the swimming men’s 4×200 m freestyle relay, with Scott adding another silver in the 4×100 m medley relay. Katherine Grainger rowed to silver in the women’s double sculls, Karen Bennett and Polly Swann did so in the women’s eight, David Florence in the canoe-slalom men’s C-2 team, and Mark Bennett and Mark Robertson in the rugby-sevens men’s tournament. Sally Conway claimed judo bronze in the women’s −70 kg category, and Eilidh Doyle earned athletics bronze in the women’s 4×400 m relay.
