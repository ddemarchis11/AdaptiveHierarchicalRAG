DA1 covers Dartford, Crayford and Barnes Cray, straddling Dartford and Bexley councils, while DA2 extends eastward to Stone, Wilmington, Bean, Hawley, Darenth and part of Joyden’s Wood under Dartford and Sevenoaks. DA3 leaves the Dartford post town, serving Longfield, Hartley, New Ash Green, New Barn and Fawkham across Dartford, Sevenoaks and Gravesham, whereas DA4 returns to Dartford post town for Farningham, Eynsford, South Darenth, Sutton-at-Hone and Horton Kirby, split between Sevenoaks and Dartford. DA5, centred on Bexley, covers Bexley Village, Blendon and slices of Albany Park and Joyden’s Wood, shared by Bexley and Dartford. Bexleyheath post town hosts DA6 for Bexleyheath and Upton wholly within Bexley, and DA7 for north Bexleyheath and Barnehurst, also Bexley-only. Erith post town handles DA8 for Erith, Northumberland Heath and Slade Green in Bexley, while Greenhithe post town covers DA9 for Greenhithe and Stone in Dartford. Swanscombe post town serves DA10 for Swanscombe and Ebbsfleet, likewise Dartford. Gravesend post town spreads across DA11 for west Gravesend and Northfleet, touching Gravesham and Dartford; DA12 for east Gravesend, Chalk, Shorne and Cobham, wholly Gravesham; and DA13 for Meopham, Istead Rise, Vigo and Southfleet, reaching into Gravesham, Dartford, Sevenoaks and a sliver of Tonbridge and Malling. Sidcup post town splits into DA14 for Sidcup, Foots Cray, North Cray, Longlands, Ruxley and part of Albany Park, spanning Bexley and Bromley, and DA15 for north Sidcup, Blackfen, Lamorbey, Longlands and part of Avery Hill, shared by Bexley and Greenwich. Welling post town covers DA16 for Welling, Falconwood and East Wickham, again Bexley and Greenwich, while Belvedere post town serves DA17 for Belvedere, Lessness Heath and part of Abbey Wood within Bexley. Finally, Erith post town returns for DA18, covering Erith Marshes and part of Thamesmead, wholly in Bexley.
