Democratic Republic of the Congo dominates the Ebony Shoe with ten victories: 1997, 2000, 2004, 2005, 2011, 2012, 2014, 2015, 2017 and 2018. Nigeria followed with five straight wins from 1992 through 1996. Morocco claimed four awards in 2006, 2008, 2009 and 2010. Ghana, Guinea, Egypt, Burkina Faso, Ivory Coast, Burundi, Senegal, Algeria and Tanzania each lifted the trophy once, respectively in 1998, 1999, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2007, 2013, 2016 and 2019.
