Super Mario 64 kicked off the North American Wii Virtual Console on 19 November 2006, followed by Mario Kart 64 on 29 January 2007 and The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time on 26 February 2007, all published by Nintendo with an E rating. Star Fox 64 arrived on 2 April 2007, F-Zero X on 25 June 2007, and Intelligent Systems’ Paper Mario on 16 July 2007, while Wave Race 64 surfaced on 6 August 2007 and Yoshi’s Story on 17 September 2007. Treasure’s T-rated Sin and Punishment debuted on 1 October 2007, and HAL Laboratory with Pax Softnica released Pokémon Snap on 10 December 2007. Nintendo’s 1080° Snowboarding landed on 28 January 2008, HAL Laboratory’s Kirby 64: The Crystal Shards on 25 February 2008, and Midway Games’ Cruis’n USA on 31 March 2008, all carrying an E rating. Pokémon Puzzle League from Nintendo Software Technology and Intelligent Systems arrived on 5 May 2008, Camelot Software’s Mario Golf on 6 October 2008, and Nintendo EAD’s The Legend of Zelda: Majora’s Mask on 18 May 2009. HAL Laboratory’s Super Smash Bros. joined on 21 December 2009, Quest and Dual Corporation’s T-rated Ogre Battle 64: Person of Lordly Caliber on 29 March 2010, Camelot’s Mario Tennis on 28 June 2010, and Hudson Soft’s Mario Party 2 closed the list on 20 December 2010.
