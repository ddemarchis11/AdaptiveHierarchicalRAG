Barcelona overtook Real Madrid to top the 2020 money league with €840.8 million, while Madrid slipped to second on €757.3 million. Manchester United stayed third on €711.5 million, Bayern Munich fourth on €660.1 million, and Paris Saint-Germain rose one place to fifth on €635.9 million. Manchester City fell to sixth on €610.6 million, Liverpool remained seventh on €604.7 million, and Tottenham Hotspur jumped two spots to eighth on €521.1 million. Chelsea dropped to ninth on €513.1 million, Juventus edged up to tenth on €459.7 million, and Arsenal tumbled two places to eleventh on €445.6 million. Borussia Dortmund, Atlético Madrid and Internazionale all held station at twelfth, thirteenth and fourteenth respectively on €377.1 million, €367.6 million and €364.6 million. Schalke 04 climbed one place to fifteenth on €324.8 million, Roma slipped to sixteenth on €231.0 million, and Lyon soared eleven places to seventeenth on €220.8 million. West Ham United rose two spots to eighteenth on €216.4 million, Everton fell to nineteenth on €213.0 million, and Napoli rounded out the top twenty on €207.4 million.
