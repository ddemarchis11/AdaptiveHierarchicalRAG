Goalkeeper Felipe, 21, left Santos for Paraná Clube, while another 21-year-old keeper named Felipe headed to Portuguesa Santista and 20-year-old Vladimir transferred to Fortaleza. Defenders Carlinhos (21) and Anderson Salles (21) moved to Mirassol and Juventus respectively, and Domingos (24) joined Portuguesa. Midfielders Dionísio (20), Wesley (22), and Adriano (21) departed for Oeste, Atlético-PR, and São Caetano, and 30-year-old Roberto Brum went to Figueirense. Forwards Mariano Trípodi (21) and Tiago Luís (20) signed with Atlético Mineiro and União Leiria. Patrik (21) and Hudson (21) each left twice, Patrik to Comercial and Uberaba, Hudson to Santa Cruz and Ituano. Filipi Souza (21) and Vinicius Simon (22) respectively joined Uberaba and Santo André.
