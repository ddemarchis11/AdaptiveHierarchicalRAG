Bang Gui-Man, Song Dae-Nam and Kwon Young-Woo swept the men’s judo golds at 73 kg, 81 kg and 90 kg, while Chung Jung-Yeon and Jeong Gyeong-Mi claimed the women’s titles at 48 kg and 78 kg. Lee Gyu-Jin and Hwang Dae-Sung added taekwondo welterweight and middleweight crowns at 78 kg and 84 kg, Kim Min-Jung and Jang Eun-Suk taking the women’s fin- and flyweight golds at 47 kg and 51 kg, and Kim Jun-Yul topped wushu men’s sanshou 56 kg. Silver medals went to judoka Hwang Ye-Sul at 70 kg and Kim Na-Young at +78 kg, karateka Lee Ji-Hwan at 60 kg, taekwondists Kim Jae-Bong at 54 kg and Lee Woorinala at 58 kg, wushu fighter Yu Hyeon-Seok at 60 kg, and wushu woman Kim A-Ri at 48 kg. Bronze medals were earned by judoka Kim Ki-Yong at 60 kg and taekwondo heavyweight Oh Min-Ah at +72 kg.
