Comply and Top Fry AllStars both make Afraha Stadium in Nakuru their home, while G.F.C. 105 play at The Discipleship Grounds in Eldoret. Kisero, Swan Orchards and Timsales are each based in an unknown stadium; Kisero and Swan Orchards list no specific town, whereas Timsales hail from Elburgon. Kolongolo, Mumcop and West Kenya Sugar share Bukhungu Stadium in Kakamega, Nzoia United occupy Sudi Stadium in Bungoma, Shabana host matches at Gusii Stadium in Kisii, and Zoo Kericho play at Green Stadium in Kericho.
