Albalonga from Albano Laziale plays at the 1,500-seat Pio XII and finished 7th in Serie D Girone G, while Arzachena, housed in the 3,100-capacity Biagio Pirina, came 6th in the same group. Avezzano ended 10th in Girone F at dei Marsi, a 3,692-seat ground, and Città di Castello took 13th in Girone E at the tiny 1,000-seat Corrado Bernicchi. Flaminia of Civita Castellana placed 9th in Girone G at Turrido Madani (1,300), Foligno 8th in Girone E at Enzo Blasone (5,650), and L’Aquila sat 16th in Lega Pro Girone B at the 7,000-seat Gran Sasso d’Italia. Lanusei finished 13th in Girone G at Lixius, Latte Dolce won Eccellenza Sardinia at Vanni Sanna (5,821), and Muravera came 12th in Girone G at Comunale. Nuorese placed 8th in Girone G at Franco Frogheri (7,000), Nuova Monterosi topped Eccellenza Lazio Girone A at Marcello Martoni, Ostia Mare 10th in Girone G at Anco Marzio (1,000), and Rieti 4th in Girone G at Centro d’Italia - Manlio Scopigno (9,980).
