Ansan Hallelujah play in Ansan at the 35,000-seat Ansan Wa~ Stadium, while Busan Transportation Corporation occupy Busan’s 24,363-capacity Gudeok Stadium. Changwon City host matches at the 15,500-seat Changwon Football Center, Cheonan City use Cheonan Stadium with 32,000 seats, and Chungju Hummel play in front of 15,000 at Chungju Stadium. Daejeon Korea Hydro & Nuclear Power take the field at Daejeon Hanbat Stadium, capacity 20,618, and Gangneung City fill Gangneung Stadium, which holds 22,333. Gimhae City’s home is the 30,000-seat Gimhae Stadium, whereas Goyang KB Kookmin Bank split their fixtures between Goyang Stadium and Uijeongbu Stadium, whose capacities are 41,311 and 35,000 respectively. Incheon Korail’s matches are staged on the 5,000-capacity practice pitch beside Incheon Munhak Stadium. Mokpo City play at the 5,952-seat Mokpo International Football Center, Suwon City alternate between the 24,670-seat Suwon Sports Complex and the 1,044-seat Suwon World Cup Stadium Auxiliary Pitch, Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dockyard Dolphin draw 19,665 to Ulsan Stadium, and Yongin City’s games are held at the 12,000-seat Yongin Football Center.
