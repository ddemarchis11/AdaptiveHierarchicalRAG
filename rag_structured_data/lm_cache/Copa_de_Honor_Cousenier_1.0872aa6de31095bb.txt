Nacional dominated the Copa de Honor Cousenier, lifting the trophy four times in 1905, 1915, 1916 and 1917. CURCC, later Peñarol, celebrated three victories in 1909, 1911 and 1918. Alumni won the 1906 edition, Belgrano Athletic Club triumphed in 1907, and Wanderers secured the 1908 title. River Plate (M) earned its lone honour in 1912, Racing followed with success in 1913, and Boca Juniors closed the competition’s history by claiming the 1920 crown.
