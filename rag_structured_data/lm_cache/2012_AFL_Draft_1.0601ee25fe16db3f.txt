Richmond and Daniel Connors parted ways by mutual consent on 4 July 2012 after he repeatedly failed to meet club expectations. Hawthorn’s Cameron Bruce retired immediately on 16 July 2012 because of a chronic back injury, while Melbourne captain Brad Green announced on 8 August that he would retire at season’s end. Richmond trio Brad Miller and Kelvin Moore joined Lindsay Gilbee and Ryan Hargrave of the Western Bulldogs in immediate retirement on 16 and 20 August respectively. Collingwood’s Chris Tarrant followed suit on 21 August, Fremantle’s Antoni Grover on 28 August, and Essendon’s Mark McVeigh the next day. Gold Coast’s Josh Fraser closed August by retiring on the 29th. September began with Western Bulldogs delisting Andrew Hooper on the 3rd while teammate James Mulligan retired the same day; Melbourne’s Liam Jurrah and Brisbane’s Amon Buchanan also left on 3 September, Jurrah resigning and Buchanan retiring. Brent Moloney resigned from Melbourne on 4 September, and Greater Western Sydney delisted Steve Clifton and Tim Segrave on 5 September while veteran James McDonald retired and St Kilda delisted Brett Peake.
