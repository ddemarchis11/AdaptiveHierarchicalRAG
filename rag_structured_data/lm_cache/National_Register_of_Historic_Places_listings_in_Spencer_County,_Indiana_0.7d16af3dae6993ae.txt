The Hardy-Baumgaertner House at 105 Walnut St. in Rockport entered the National Register on 29 November 2019. Huffman Mill Covered Bridge, carrying County Road 1490N across the Anderson River east of Fulda within Harrison Township, was listed 1 April 1998. Colonel William Jones House sits west of Gentryville on Troy-Vincennes Road in Jackson Township and gained recognition 12 May 1975. Lincoln Boyhood National Memorial, straddling Carter and Clay Townships along State Road 162 southeast of Lincoln City, was enrolled 15 October 1966. Lincoln Pioneer Village, at the junction of 9th Street and Eureka Road in Rockport, followed on 20 April 1998. The Rockport Historic District, roughly bounded by First, Seminary, Greenwood, Lincoln, Eighth, William, and Pearl Streets, was designated 28 August 2019. St. Boniface Church stands on State Road 545 at Fulda in Harrison Township and was listed 23 October 1980. The Mathias Sharp House, 319 S. 2nd St. in Rockport, joined the Register 16 June 1983. Spencer County Courthouse, occupying the block bounded by 2nd, 3rd, Main, and Walnut Streets in Rockport, was added 12 March 1999.
