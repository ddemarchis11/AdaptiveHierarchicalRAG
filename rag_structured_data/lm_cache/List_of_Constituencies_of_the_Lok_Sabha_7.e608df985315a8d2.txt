Kachchh, numbered 1, is a Scheduled Caste seat, while Banaskantha (2), Patan (3), Mahesana (4), Sabarkantha (5), Gandhinagar (6), Ahmedabad East (7), Surendranagar (9), Rajkot (10), Porbandar (11), Jamnagar (12), Junagadh (13), Amreli (14), Bhavnagar (15), Anand (16), Kheda (17), Panchmahal (18) and Vadodara (20) are open contests. Ahmedabad West, at position 8, is also reserved for Scheduled Castes, and Dahod, nineteenth in order, is the single Scheduled Tribe constituency in this segment.
