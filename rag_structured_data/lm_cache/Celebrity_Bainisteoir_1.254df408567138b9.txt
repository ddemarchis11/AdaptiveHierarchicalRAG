Radio presenter Ray D'Arcy took charge of Rathangan in Kildare, while journalist John Waters managed St Michael’s of Cootehall in Roscommon. Comedian-actress Katherine Lynch guided St Patrick’s, Dromahair in Leitrim, and The Den’s children’s TV host Emma O’Driscoll led St Patrick’s of Limerick. Rugby analyst George Hook handled Cuala in Dublin, model Andrea Roche oversaw Rockwell Rovers in Tipperary, TV presenter John McGuire directed Sneem in Kerry, and fellow broadcaster Derek Davis steered Glasdrumman in Down.
