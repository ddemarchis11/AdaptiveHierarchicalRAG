11 Freunde covers German association football monthly, while the United Kingdom’s weekly Autosport follows motorsport. The United States fields two baseball titles: Baseball America every two weeks and Baseball Digest eight times yearly. Basketball Times appears monthly in the United States, joined there by Dime Magazine ten times a year and the bi-monthly Hoop. The United Kingdom offers weekly Boxing News and monthly Boxing Monthly; Fight! Magazine brings American readers monthly MMA coverage. FourFourTwo and France Football serve monthly and bi-weekly association-football audiences from the United Kingdom and France respectively, Germany’s Kicker also ships bi-weekly, and the United Kingdom’s weekly Match rounds out the football group. Golf Magazine tees off monthly in the United States, The Hockey News slides onto Canadian newsstands weekly, Lacrosse Magazine publishes monthly stateside, Pro Football Weekly tackles the NFL weekly in the United States, and Pro Wrestling Illustrated grapples for monthly attention there too.
