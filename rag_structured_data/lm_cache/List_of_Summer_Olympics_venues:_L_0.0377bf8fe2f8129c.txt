La Romareda in Barcelona hosted 1992 Olympic football for 43,000 spectators, while Laakso outside Helsinki staged 1952 eventing riding for 12,500. Lagoa Rodrigo de Freitas welcomed Rio 2016 canoe sprint and rowing to 14,000 seats, and Lake Albano outside Rome did the same for 10,000 in 1960. Lake Casitas served Los Angeles 1984 canoe and rowing to 4,680, Lake Lanier carried Atlanta 1996 canoe sprint and rowing to 17,300, Lake Sagami held Tokyo 1964 canoe for 1,500, and Lake Wendouree hosted Melbourne 1956 canoe and rowing for 14,300. Lahti’s 82,000-capacity ground handled 1952 Helsinki football, and Lansdowne Park welcomed 30,000 for Montréal 1976 football. Beijing 2008 built three Laoshan venues: a 4,000-seat BMX circuit, a 2,000-seat mountain-bike course, and a 6,000-seat track velodrome. L’Aquila Communal Stadium held 9,285 for Rome 1960 football, while Lazio Pigeon Shooting Stand seated 2,000 for 1960 shotgun-trap events. Le Havre provided sailing courses for Paris 1900 and again in 1924, the latter listing 541. Le Stade Olympique Reims seated 420 for 1924 trap and running-target shooting, and Le Stand de Tir de Versailles offered 82 places for 1924 modern-pentathlon shooting plus 25 m rapid-fire pistol and running deer. Lee Valley White Water Centre carried 12,000 for London 2012 canoe slalom.
