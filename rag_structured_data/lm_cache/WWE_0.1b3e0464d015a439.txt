Baron Corbin became 2019 King of the Ring on September 16 in Knoxville by defeating Chad Gable in the tournament final. That same May 19 in Hartford, Brock Lesnar captured the men’s Money in the Bank briefcase after outlasting Baron Corbin, Drew McIntyre, Ali, Andrade, Randy Orton, Ricochet and Finn Bálor, while Bayley won the women’s ladder match over Carmella, Dana Brooke, Ember Moon, Mandy Rose, Naomi, Natalya and Nikki Cross. Drew McIntyre punched his ticket to WrestleMania by winning the 2020 men’s Royal Rumble in Houston on January 26, last ousting Roman Reigns, and Charlotte Flair claimed the women’s Rumble that same night by lastly eliminating Shayna Baszler. In Winter Park on January 29, 2020, The BroserWeights—Matt Riddle and Pete Dunne—took the Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic trophy by beating The Grizzled Young Veterans in the final. Braun Strowman won the André the Giant Memorial Battle Royal at WrestleMania 35 in East Rutherford on April 7, 2019, last dumping Colin Jost, and Carmella won the WrestleMania Women’s Battle Royal there by lastly dispatching Sarah Logan. Finally, The O.C.—Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson—won the 2019 WWE World Cup tag turmoil in Riyadh on October 31, lastly ousting the Viking Raiders.
