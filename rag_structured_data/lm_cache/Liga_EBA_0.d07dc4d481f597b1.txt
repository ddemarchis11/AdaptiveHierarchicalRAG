AC1+ Piélagos represents Renedo de Piélagos at Fernando Expósito arena in Cantabria, while fellow Cantabrians Arha Hoteles play in Santander’s Palacio de Deportes and CB Solares calls Medio Cudeyo’s Mies del Corro home. Baskonia B, the reserve side from Vitoria-Gasteiz, hosts games at Mendizorrotza in the Basque Country, joined there by CB Santurtzi SK at Mikel Trueba in Santurtzi, Easo at José Antonio Gasca in San Sebastián, Goierri-Iparragirre 2020 at Aldiri in Urretxu, Mondragón Unibersitatea at Iturripe in Mondragón, Tabirako Baqué at Landako in Durango, and Ulacia Zarautz at Aritzbatalde in Zarautz. Castile and León supplies CB La Flecha, who compete in Arroyo de la Encomienda’s La Vega, Manteneo Filipenses in Palencia’s Municipal gym, and Nissan Grupo de Santiago at El Plantío in Burgos. Rounding out the group, Megacalzado Ardoi plays in the Municipal arena of Zizur Mayor-Zizur Nagusia, Navarre.
