Q-Games released Digidrive for Game Boy Advance on July 27, 2006 as part of the Bit Generations series, then ported it to DSiWare on October 2, 2009 under the title Art Style: DIGIDRIVE. Star Fox Command arrived for Nintendo DS on August 28, 2006, followed by the 3DS remaster Star Fox 64 3D on September 9, 2011, co-developed with Nintendo EAD. The PixelJunk line began with Racers on September 13, 2007 for PlayStation 3, then Monsters on January 24, 2008, which later spread to PlayStation Vita, Windows, Mac and Linux; its expansion Monsters Encore landed April 24, 2008, while the PSP compilation Monsters Deluxe released October 1, 2009. Eden debuted July 31, 2008 on PlayStation 3 and Windows, Shooter arrived December 10, 2009 and ultimately reached PS3, Windows, Mac, Linux, PS4 and Vita, spawning the sequel Shooter 2 on March 1, 2011 and the combined Shooter Ultimate on October 21, 2015. SideScroller followed October 22, 2011, 4am on May 15, 2012, and Visualizer on August 13, 2013. DSiWare saw Trajectile (aka Reflect Missile) November 24, 2009, Starship Defence (Starship Patrol) December 18, 2009, and X-Scape (X Returns/3D Space Tank) May 31, 2010. Nom Nom Galaxy launched August 3, 2015 for Windows, Mac, Linux and PS4, while the free-to-play social-action hybrid The Tomorrow Children reached PlayStation 4 on September 6, 2016.
