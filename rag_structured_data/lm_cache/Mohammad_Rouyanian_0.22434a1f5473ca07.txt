Saeid Ghadami arrived from Foolad in 2011 as the first signing, followed the same year by Éamon Zayed from Derry City. Mehdi Mahdavikia joined from Damash Gilan in 2012, the same year Hossein Hooshyar came from Shahrdari Yasuj and Hossein Kanaani was promoted from the youth system. 2012 also saw Mehrdad Pooladi arrive from Mes Kerman, Bosnian keeper Asmir Avdukić from Borac Banja Luka, and a trio from Zob Ahan: Mohammad Ghazi, Shahab Gordan and Hossein Mahini. Jalal Hosseini and Mohsen Bengar both moved from Sepahan in 2012, while Mohammad Reza Khanzadeh came from Rah Ahan, Afshin Esmaeilzadeh from Damash, and Karim Ansarifard from Saipa. Brazilian midfielder Roberto Sousa was signed from Marítimo, Amir Abedzadeh returned from LA Blues, Mehrzad Madanchi arrived from UAE side Al-Shaab, and goalkeeper Nilson Corrêa came from Portuguese club Guimarães, all in 2012. Reza Haghighi completed the sequence, joining from Fajr Sepasi in 2013.
