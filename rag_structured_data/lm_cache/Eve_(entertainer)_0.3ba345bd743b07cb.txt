In 2002 Eve debuted on screen as J.J. in *XXX* and immediately followed as Terri Jones in *Barbershop*. She cameoed as herself in 2003’s *Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle*, then 2004 saw her portray Mary-Kay in *The Woodsman*, reprise Terri Jones in *Barbershop 2: Back in Business*, and play Becky in *The Cookout*. After appearing as Ophelia Franklin in 2008’s *Flashbacks of a Fool*, she surfaced as herself in 2009’s *Good Hair* and took the role of Rosa Sparks in *Whip It*. She stepped into Latisha for 2010’s *4.3.2.1*, then twice joined the *Gumball 3000* documentaries as herself in 2011 and 2012, the same year she played Natalie in *All Wifed Out*. She closed out with Mocha Sujata in 2013’s *Bounty Killer*, Barbara in 2014’s *Animal*, and returned once more as Terri Jones in 2016’s *Barbershop: The Next Cut*.
