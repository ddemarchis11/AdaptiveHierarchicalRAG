Kenya’s six Beijing golds came from Pamela Jelimo in the women’s 800 m, Brimin Kipruto in the men’s 3000 m steeplechase, Wilfred Bungei in the men’s 800 m, Nancy Lagat in the women’s 1500 m, Samuel Wanjiru in the men’s marathon and Asbel Kiprop in the men’s 1500 m. Silver medals were earned by Janeth Jepkosgei in the women’s 800 m, Catherine Ndereba in the women’s marathon, Eunice Jepkorir in the women’s 3000 m steeplechase and Eliud Kipchoge in the men’s 5000 m. Bronze medals went to Richard Mateelong in the men’s 3000 m steeplechase, Micah Kogo in the men’s 10 000 m, Alfred Kirwa Yego in the men’s 800 m, Edwin Cheruiyot Soi in the men’s 5000 m, Sylvia Kibet in the women’s 5000 m and Linet Masai in the women’s 10 000 m.
