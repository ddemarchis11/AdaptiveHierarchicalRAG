In the first round Minnesota claimed Florida defensive tackle Sharrif Floyd at 23rd overall, followed immediately by Florida State cornerback Xavier Rhodes at 25th overall and Tennessee wide receiver Cordarrelle Patterson at 29th overall. The Vikings waited until the fourth round to add Penn State linebacker Gerald Hodges with the 120th pick, then doubled up on UCLA specialists by selecting punter Jeff Locke 155th overall and guard Jeff Baca 196th overall. They closed the draft by choosing Penn State linebacker Michael Mauti 213th overall, North Carolina guard Travis Bond 214th overall, and Florida State defensive tackle Everett Dawkins 229th overall, giving them two linebackers, two guards, and two defensive tackles among their nine selections.
