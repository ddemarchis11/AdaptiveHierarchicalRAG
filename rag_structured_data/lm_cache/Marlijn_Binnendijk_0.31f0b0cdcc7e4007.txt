On 30 July 2004 Marlijn Binnendijk contested the individual pursuit at the Junior Coupe du Monde in Los Angeles, United States. She returned to the same event on 19 February 2005, this time racing the individual pursuit at the Track Cycling World Cup in Sydney, Australia. During the 2006 Dutch National Track Championships held in Alkmaar, Netherlands, she rode three events: the scratch race on 28 December, and both the points race and individual pursuit on 30 December. Switching to the road, she competed in the national road cycling championships on 13 June 2007, finishing the road race in Maastricht, Netherlands. Back on the track in Alkmaar, she entered the points race of the 2007 Dutch National Track Championships on 30 December 2007. Finally, on 17 February 2008, she joined the team pursuit at the Track Cycling World Cup in Ballerup, Denmark.
