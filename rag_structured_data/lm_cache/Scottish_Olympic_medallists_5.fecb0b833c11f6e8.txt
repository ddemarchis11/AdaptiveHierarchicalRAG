Andrew Lindsay struck gold for Scotland in the men’s eight rowing, while Shirley Robertson topped the women’s Europe class sailing and Stephanie Cook claimed the women’s modern pentathlon title. Chris Hoy and Craig MacLean sped to silver together in the men’s team sprint on the track, Ian Stark anchored the silver-medal three-day-event equestrian squad, and Gillian Lindsay and Katherine Grainger powered the women’s quadruple sculls to second place. Mark Covell added another Scottish silver in the open Star class sailing.
