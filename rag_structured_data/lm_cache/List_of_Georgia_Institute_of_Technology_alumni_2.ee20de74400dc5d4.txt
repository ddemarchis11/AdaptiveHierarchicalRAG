Brown, 1989 alumnus, heads Inspire Brands. Gary C. Butler, 1968 graduate, is CEO of Automatic Data Processing. Brook Byers, also 1968, is a Kleiner Perkins venture capitalist. George G. Crawford, 1890 alumnus, once headed the Tennessee Coal, Iron and Railroad Company. Quentin Dastugue, 1977 graduate, founded Property One and served four terms in the Louisiana House. Cecil B. Day, 1958 alumnus, created Days Inn Hotels. David Dorman, 1975 graduate, is chairman and CEO emeritus of AT&T. Mike Duke, 1971 alumnus, formerly ran Wal-Mart Stores. Frank Gordy, 1929 alumnus, founded The Varsity, the world’s largest drive-in. James Gerald Gulliver, 1950 graduate, built Argyll Foods into a UK retail giant.
