Center Ridge Cemetery, entered on the National Register on 8 August 2018, occupies 704 W. Johnson St. in Hamilton Township. The Daugherty-Monroe Archaeological Site (12SU13), listed 12 June 1985, lies in the northern half of Section 21, Town 8 North Range 11 West within Turman Township. Merom Site and Fort Azatlan, registered 12 May 1975, stands north of Merom and is shared by Gill Township and the town itself. Merom-Gill Township Carnegie Library, added 11 March 2019, sits at 8554 Market St. in Merom. Shakamak State Park Historic District, enrolled 15 March 2000, centers on 6265 W. State Road 48 west of Jasonville in Jackson Township. Shelburn Interurban Depot-THI & E Interurban Depot, listed 15 December 2015, stands at 3 N. Railroad St. in Shelburn. The Sherman Building, entered 22 September 1986, occupies 2-4 S. Court St. in Sullivan. Sullivan County Courthouse, registered 22 December 2008, anchors 100 Courthouse Square in Sullivan. Sullivan Courthouse Square Historic District, added 4 September 2018, roughly spans the blocks bounded by Section, Wall, State, and Harris Sts. in Sullivan. Sullivan County Poor Home, listed 15 March 2000, stands at 1447 County Road 75N east of Sullivan in Hamilton Township. Union Christian College, enrolled 25 June 1982, occupies the corner of 3rd and Philip Sts. in Merom.
