Boeung Ket Rubber Field, Kirivong Sok Sen Chey and Phnom Penh Crown all wear Kappa kits, led respectively by captains Keo Sokngon, In Vicheka and Kouch Sokumpheak and coached by Prak Sovuthy, Nguyen Chov Hoang and Englishman David Booth. National Defense Ministry and Preah Khan Reach are Nike-outfitted sides, with Khek Khemrin captaining the military team under Op Sam Ath and Sok Rithy skippering Preah Khan Reach for Som Vandeth. National Police Commissary sport Joma kits, captain Say Piseth and coach Ung Kangyanith, while Nagacorp FC have Om Thavrak as skipper and Prak Sovannara on the bench. Build Bright United’s captain Dan Ito plays for Meas Sam Oeurn, and Chhlam Samuth’s Silva Sunday takes orders from Kim Pheakdey.
