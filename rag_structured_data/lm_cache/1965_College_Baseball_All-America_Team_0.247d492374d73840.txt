The 1965 College Baseball All-America Team honored Ohio State’s Steve Arlin and Saint Louis’ John Marcum Jr. as the nation’s top pitchers, while Washington State’s John E. Olerud claimed the catcher spot. Stanford’s Pete Middlekauff earned first-base recognition, Arizona State’s Luis Lagunas took second base, Texas A&M’s Mike McClure manned third, and Columbia’s Archie Roberts held down shortstop. Outfield accolades went to Western Michigan’s Bill Guerrant, Cincinnati’s Billy Wolff, and Arizona State’s Rick Monday.
