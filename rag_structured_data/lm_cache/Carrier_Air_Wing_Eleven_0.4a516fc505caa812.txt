Carrier Air Wing Eleven embarks aboard USS Theodore Roosevelt with nine squadrons: the Black Knights of VFA-154 fly F/A-18F Super Hornets, while the Tomcatters of VFA-31, Blue Diamonds of VFA-146, and Golden Warriors of VFA-87 all operate single-seat F/A-18E Super Hornets. Overhead, the Liberty Bells of VAW-115 keep watch in their E-2C Hawkeyes, and the Gray Wolves of VAQ-142 jam enemy radars with EA-18G Growlers. Logistical lifelines are maintained by the Providers of VRC-30, Detachment 3, flying C-2A Greyhounds, while rotary-wing missions split between the Eightballers of HSC-8 with MH-60S Seahawks and the Wolfpack of HSM-75 wielding MH-60R Seahawks for maritime strike.
