Matthew Rhys debuted on screen in 1997 as Boyo in House of America, then played Demetrius in Titus and Ray Smith in Whatever Happened to Harold Smith? both in 1999. He followed with Carl in Sorted (2000), Nob in Very Annie Mary (2001), James Strang in The Abduction Club, Doc Fairweather in Deathwatch and Eddie in Shooters (all 2002), and the Welsh mythic hero Lleu Llaw Gyffes in Y Mabinogi (2003). After portraying Nick Edwards in Fakers (2004), Rhys took the roles of Peter Simon in Love and Other Disasters (2006), Count Dzerzhinsky in Virgin Territory (2007), Dylan Thomas in The Edge of Love (2008), Marc in The Think Tank (2009), Mateo in Patagonia (2010), Damien in Everything Carries Me To You (2011) and the dual role of John Standing and Johnny Spence in The Scapegoat (2012).
