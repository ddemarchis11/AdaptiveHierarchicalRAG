Nandurbar is the first Lok Sabha seat, reserved for Scheduled Tribes, followed by Dhule, Jalgaon, Raver, Buldhana, Akola, Wardha, Nagpur, Bhandara-Gondiya, Chandrapur, Yavatmal-Washim, Hingoli, Nanded, Parbhani, Jalna and Aurangabad, all open constituencies. Amravati and Ramtek are Scheduled Caste constituencies, while Gadchiroli-Chimur and Dindori are the other two Scheduled Tribe seats among these twenty.
