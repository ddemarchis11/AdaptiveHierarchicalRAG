BBC Park Western in Acton doubles as the production base for Silent Witness and the hub of BBC Newsgathering Operations handling outside broadcasts, while the BBC Elstree Centre in Borehamwood serves as a dedicated studio centre. On Great Portland Street, BBC Great Portland Street houses the BBC Trust, next-door Wogan House—once called Western House—and Yalding House, formerly the home of BBC Radio 1 and BBC 1Xtra. Broadcasting House on Langham Place is the corporation’s headquarters and hosts both BBC News and BBC Television. Maida Vale’s BBC Maida Vale is a studio centre that shelters the BBC Symphony Orchestra and its recording studios, and the BBC Archive Centre in Perivale safeguards the BBC Archives. At the Southbank Centre the BBC leases small technical spaces in both the Queen Elizabeth Hall and the Royal Festival Hall to record events. Four Millbank in Westminster provides a parliamentary studio leased from ITV, White City’s BBC Centre House contains the BBC Research South Lab, the neighbouring BBC Television Centre—once the heart of BBC Television—now mostly sold yet still partly occupied by BBC Worldwide, BBC Studios staff and BBC Studioworks, while the BBC White City complex, comprising the Broadcast Centre and Lighthouse, functions as a large office and production centre.
