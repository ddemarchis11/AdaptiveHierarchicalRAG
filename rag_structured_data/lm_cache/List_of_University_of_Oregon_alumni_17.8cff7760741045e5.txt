Amir Aczel earned a 1982 Ph.D. and writes about science and mathematics, while Raymond Delacy Adams, a 1933 bachelors graduate, became a neurologist and Fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences. Geoffrey R. Ball, a physiology alumnus, co-founded Symphonix Devices Inc., pioneer of middle-ear implants. David M. Beazley completed his 1993 M.S. and is a computer scientist, Python developer and author; Kent Beck, holding 1987 B.S. and M.S. degrees, created Extreme Programming and Test Driven Development. Pamela J. Bjorkman, 1971 B.A. biochemist, is a Caltech Max Delbruck Professor and x-ray crystallography pioneer. Richard Gordon, 1967 Ph.D. theoretical biologist, advanced image processing at the University of Manitoba. Walter Houser Brattain, 1926 M.A., co-invented the transistor and shared the 1956 Nobel Prize in Physics. Clifford E. Brubaker, 1968 Ph.D., founded and once led the Rehabilitation Engineering and Assistive Technology Society of North America, and Robert S. Cunningham earned 1969 M.S. and Ph.D. degrees before becoming CSU Stanislaus computer-science professor emeritus. Stanislas Dehaene, a postdoc alumnus, researches numerical-cognition neuroscience, and 1982 Ph.D. Anne Fernald is a Stanford psychologist. N. Gregory Hamilton, 1971 B.A., is a psychiatrist advocating assisted suicide. Douglas Hofstadter, M.S. 1972 and Ph.D. 1975, won the 1980 Pulitzer for general non-fiction with “Gödel, Escher, Bach” and teaches cognitive science. Anthony Leiserowitz, M.S. and Ph.D. graduate, directs Yale’s Project on Climate Change Communication. Daniel Levitin earned 1993 M.S. and 1996 Ph.D. degrees and works as a cognitive scientist. Esther Pohl Lovejoy, 1894 M.D., was an early female physician and suffrage activist. William Murphy, 1914 B.A., shared the 1934 Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine. PZ Myers, 1985 Ph.D., is a prominent biologist and science blogger, and Harry F. Noller, also 1985 Ph.D., directs UC Santa Cruz’s Center for the Molecular Biology of RNA, is a National Academy of Sciences member, and elucidated ribosome function.
