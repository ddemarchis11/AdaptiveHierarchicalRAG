Georgia’s offense revolved around sophomore quarterback Aaron Murray, protected by senior tackle Cordy Glenn, with junior tight end Orson Charles as a prime target. In the backfield, South Carolina featured sophomore Marcus Lattimore, while Alabama countered with junior Trent Richardson. The receiving corps paired Arkansas senior Greg Childs with South Carolina junior Alshon Jeffery. Up front, Alabama’s line was anchored by junior Barrett Jones and senior center William Vlachos, flanked by Mississippi senior Bradley Sowell and Kentucky junior Larry Warford.
