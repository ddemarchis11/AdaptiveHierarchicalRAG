Carrier Air Wing Five fields nine squadrons: VFA-27 Royal Maces, VFA-102 Diamondbacks, VFA-115 Eagles and VFA-195 Dambusters each fly variants of the F/A-18 Super Hornet, with the Royal Maces and both Eagles and Dambusters on single-seat F/A-18Es while the Diamondbacks operate the two-seat F/A-18F. VAW-125 Tiger Tails provide airborne early warning with E-2D Hawkeyes, VAQ-141 Shadowhawks bring electronic attack capability via EA-18G Growlers, and VRC-30 Det. 5 Providers handle carrier onboard delivery using C-2A Greyhounds. Rotary-wing assets are split between HSC-12 Golden Falcons flying MH-60S Seahawks for sea combat support and HSM-77 Saberhawks operating MH-60R Seahawks for maritime strike missions.
