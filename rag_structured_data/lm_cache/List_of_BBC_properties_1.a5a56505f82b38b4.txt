The BBC’s local presence stretches from Barnsley Digital Media Centre, which serves as the Barnsley studio for BBC Radio Sheffield, to the Barnstaple Civic Centre housing BBC Radio Devon’s studio. Barrow-in-Furness hosts BBC Broadcasting House (Cumbria) for BBC Radio Cumbria, while Basingstoke College of Technology provides the local studio for BBC Radio Berkshire. In Bath, BBC Bath supports BBC Radio Bristol, and Bedford County Hall is home to BBC Three Counties Radio. Birmingham’s Mailbox is the base for BBC WM, complemented by the BBC Drama Village in the same city. Blackburn’s BBC Blackburn serves BBC Radio Lancashire, and Boston Business Centre hosts the local studio for BBC Lincolnshire. Bradford’s National Media Museum contains the BBC Radio Leeds studio, while BBC Brighton operates BBC Sussex. Bristol’s BBC Broadcasting House (Bristol) is shared with BBC Radio Bristol, and Bromsgrove District Council House supports BBC Hereford and Worcester. Burton upon Trent Town Hall hosts the local studio for BBC Radio Derby, Cambridge’s BBC Broadcasting House (Cambridge) is for BBC Radio Cambridgeshire, and Canterbury Christ Church University provides the local studio for BBC Radio Kent. Carlisle’s BBC Broadcasting House (Carlisle) also serves BBC Radio Cumbria, Chatham’s Sun Pier Chambers supports BBC Radio Kent, and Chelmsford’s BBC Broadcasting House (Chelmsford) is home to BBC Essex.
