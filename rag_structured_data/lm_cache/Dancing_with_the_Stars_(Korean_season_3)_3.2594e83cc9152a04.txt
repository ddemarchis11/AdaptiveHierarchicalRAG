Fei dominated the season, topping five dances: she scored 28 for both Samba and Paso Doble, 27 for Jive, 26 for Rumba, 22 for Quickstep and a season-high 29 for Cha-cha-cha. Lee Jong-won opened with a 21-point Swing but later sank to 19 for Samba and 21 for Tango. Nam Bo-ra peaked at 22 in Viennese Waltz yet bottom-scored 19 in Jive, Tango and Paso Doble. Kim Wan-sun’s 25-point Tango stood as that dance’s best, while Seungho’s 22-point Waltz earned top marks before he slipped to 19 in Viennese Waltz. Lee Eun-gyeol claimed Salsa with 24 and shared the 29-point Latin-based Freestyle spotlight with Fei, also posting 26 for Fusion. Hye Park’s 20-point Foxtrot was best in that style, leaving her at 19 for Rumba. Woo Ji-Won managed 19 in Jive and a low 17 in Quickstep. Kim Dae-ho repeatedly landed at the bottom—18 in Foxtrot, 20 in Cha-cha-cha, 21 in Tango and Fusion—while Oh Mi-hee scored 19 for both Waltz and Cha-cha-cha, Jeanette Lee hit 18 in Salsa, and Kim Kyung-ho ended with 19 in Jive and 26 in Latin-based Freestyle.
