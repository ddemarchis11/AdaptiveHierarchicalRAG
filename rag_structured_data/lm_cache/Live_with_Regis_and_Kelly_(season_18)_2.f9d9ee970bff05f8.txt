Regis Philbin and Kelly Ripa opened November with Billy Crystal and Mike Wallace on the 1st, followed the next day by Terrence Howard, Philip Seymour Hoffman and Kirk Franklin. James Spader visited on November 3, Jake Gyllenhaal on the 4th, and Josh Duhamel shared the couch with Simon Cowell on the 7th. 50 Cent and Peter Gallagher appeared November 8, while November 9 brought Melina Kanakaredes, LeAnn Rimes and Keira Knightley together. Robert Downey Jr., Carrot Top and Daniel Radcliffe formed an eclectic November 10 panel, and Steve Martin, Nicole Richie plus Anderson Cooper closed the second week on the 11th. Richard Gere, Donald Sutherland and Joy Philbin chatted on November 14, Reese Witherspoon and Emma Watson on the 15th, Joaquin Phoenix alongside Jesse L. Martin on the 16th. Rosario Dawson stopped by November 17, Patricia Arquette on the 18th, Usher and Alanis Morissette on the 21st. Judge Judy Sheindlin and Bradley Cooper visited November 22, a full Seinfeld reunion—Jerry Seinfeld, Julia Louis-Dreyfus, Jason Alexander and Michael Richards—convened on the 23rd, Lake Bell, John Leguizamo and Jeff Garlin appeared the 25th, Felicity Huffman and NASCAR’s Carl Edwards on the 28th, and Barbara Walters, Tom Arnold and Scott Stapp wrapped the month on November 29.
