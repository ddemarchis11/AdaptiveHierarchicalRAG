Bacillus anthracis causes anthrax and is prevented by the BioThrax anthrax vaccine. Bordetella pertussis triggers whooping cough and is targeted by the DPT vaccine, sold as Boostrix, Adacel, Daptacel, Infanrix, Tripedia, Kinrix, Pediarix, Pentacel, Tetramune, Quinvaxem, Pentavac PFS, Easyfive TT, Shan-5, and ComBE Five. Clostridium tetani produces tetanus, Corynebacterium diphtheriae causes diphtheria, and both are likewise covered by the same DPT vaccine brands plus Decavac and Tenivac. Coxiella burnetii leads to Q fever and is blocked by the Q-Vax Q fever vaccine. Haemophilus influenzae type B provokes epiglottitis, meningitis, and pneumonia, and is countered by the Hib vaccine available as Hiberix, Pentacel, ActHIB, Pedvax HIB, Tetramune, Quinvaxem, Pentavac PFS, Easyfive TT, Shan-5, and ComBE Five. Mycobacterium tuberculosis causes tuberculosis and is addressed by the Tice BCG tuberculosis vaccine. Neisseria meningitidis provokes meningococcal meningitis; serotype C vaccines are Neisvac C and Meningitec, serotypes A/C/W-135/Y are covered by Mencevax, Nimenrix, Menveo, and Menactra, while serotype B is prevented by Bexsero. Salmonella typhi brings typhoid fever and is thwarted by Typhim Vi, Typherix, and Ty21a typhoid vaccines. Streptococcus pneumoniae causes pneumococcal pneumonia and is met by Pneumovax and Prevnar pneumococcal conjugate and polysaccharide vaccines. Vibrio cholerae triggers cholera and is checked by Dukoral and Shanchol cholera vaccines.
