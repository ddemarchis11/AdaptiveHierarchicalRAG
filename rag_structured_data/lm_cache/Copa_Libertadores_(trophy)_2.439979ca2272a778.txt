Boca Juniors have lifted the Copa Libertadores six times, in 1977, 1978, 2000, 2001, 2003 and 2007. River Plate have four titles, earned in 1986, 1996, 2015 and 2018. Olimpia, São Paulo and Grêmio each have three: Olimpia in 1979, 1990 and 2002; São Paulo in 1992, 1993 and 2005; Grêmio in 1983, 1995 and 2017. Independiente won twice, in 1975 and 1984, as did Cruzeiro (1976, 1997), Nacional (1980, 1988), Peñarol (1982, 1987), Atlético Nacional (1989, 2016), Internacional (2006, 2010) and Flamengo (1981, 2019). Argentinos Juniors claimed their lone trophy in 1985, Colo-Colo in 1991, Vélez Sársfield in 1994, Vasco da Gama in 1998, Palmeiras in 1999, Once Caldas in 2004, LDU Quito in 2008 and Estudiantes in 2009.
