Raghunath Mohapatra, Sonal Mansingh, Rakesh Sinha and Ram Shakal were all nominated on 14 July 2018 as BJP voices in art, literature and social work, each holding the seat until 13 July 2024. Sambhaji Raje, carrying the BJP banner for social work, began his term earlier, on 13 June 2016, and will retire on 3 May 2022. Roopa Ganguly, Suresh Gopi and Subramanian Swamy, all BJP members, entered the house on 25 April 2016 for contributions to art and economics, with their terms ending on 24 April 2022. Narendra Jadhav, Mary Kom and Swapan Dasgupta, though nominated without party labels, share the same 25 April 2016 start and 24 April 2022 retirement dates, representing economics, sport and journalism respectively. K. T. S. Tulsi, nominated for law, is the senior-most, appointed on 25 February 2014 and retiring on 24 February 2020.
