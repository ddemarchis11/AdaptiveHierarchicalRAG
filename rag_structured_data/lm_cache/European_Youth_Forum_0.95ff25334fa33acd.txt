Carina Autengruber of Austria led the European Youth Forum as president during 2019-2020, preceded by Spain’s Luis Alvarado Martínez for 2017-2018 and Finland’s Johanna Nyman for 2015-2016. Slovenia’s Peter Matjašič held the presidency across four years, 2011-2014, immediately after fellow Slovene Tine Radinja’s 2009-2010 term. Austria’s Bettina Schwarzmayr headed the forum in 2007-2008, following Lithuania’s Renaldas Vaisbrodas for 2005-2006 and Italy’s Giacomo Filibeck for 2003-2004. Finland’s Henrik Söderman presided in 2001-2002, Spain’s Pau Solanilla in 1999-2000, and Finland’s Pauliina Arola in 1997-1998.
