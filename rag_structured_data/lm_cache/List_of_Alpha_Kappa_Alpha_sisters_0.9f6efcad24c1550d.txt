Elmer Lucille Allen, initiated at Beta Epsilon chapter, became a ceramic artist and one of the first African-American female chemists in the United States. Barbara Chase-Riboud, a Delta Mu sister, gained recognition as both a sculptor and author. Kristye Addison Dudley, from Lambda Theta, works as a visual artist and painter. Honorary member Jan Spivey Gilchrist built her reputation as an illustrator. Anita Posey Lowe, initiated through Nu chapter, specializes in ceramic art. Barbara Madden-Swain, an Alpha Omicron Omega sister, also practices ceramic artistry. Laura Wheeler Waring, honored with honorary membership, created art as a painter and writer. Meta Vaux Warrick Fuller, another honorary member, established herself as an artist and sculptor. Mary Park Washington, from Kappa Omega chapter, works across multiple mediums as a painter, illustrator, and collage artist.
