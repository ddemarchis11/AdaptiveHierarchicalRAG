Anthony Gelling, a centre-cum-second-rower who played for the Sydney Roosters NYC in 2010, joined the Warriors alongside Darin Kingi, a hooker from the Manurewa Marlins, and Johnny Aranga, a fullback-winger recruited from the Mt Albert Lions. Prop-second-rower Peter-George Rata arrived from the Waicoa Bay Stallions, while versatile Sema Mataora, who can lock or play halfback, came from the Otahuhu Leopards. Shaun Metcalf, a centre-second-rower who spent 2010 with the Auckland Vulcans, was joined by Sonny Walker, a winger-centre from the Pukekohe Pythons, and Tulson Caird, a second-row-lock forward who previously played for the Te Atatu Roosters.
