Former footballer Carlos Kukín Flores and his dreamer Victoria Cordero were the first to leave on June 11, 2011, followed by actress-vedette Leysi Suárez with Eder Bocanegra six days later. Actress-TV host Mónica Hoyos and Edson del Carpio exited third on July 9, while race-car driver Mario Hart and Diana Burga were eliminated fourth on July 23. That same night saw the departure of actor-TV host-screenwriter Aldo Miyashiro and Isabel Acevedo. Actress Vanessa Terkes and Andy Sandoval secured third place, singer-model-actress Leslie Shaw with Kevin Ubillus claimed runner-up, and actor-film director Raúl Zuazo alongside Dayana Calla were crowned champions on July 23, 2011.
