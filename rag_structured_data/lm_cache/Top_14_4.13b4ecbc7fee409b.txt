Vincent Clerc tops the all-time try chart with 101 touchdowns amassed for Grenoble, Toulouse and Toulon between 2002 and 2018. One behind him, Laurent Arbo crossed 100 times while turning out for Pau, Castres, Montpellier and Perpignan across 1991-2007. Aurélien Rougerie’s entire 1999-2018 career at Clermont yielded 96 tries, good for third. Fellow Clermont star Napolioni Nalaga added 87 in two spells there and at Lyon from 2006-2017. Still active Toulouse full-back Maxime Medard has 81 since 2004, while Montpellier’s Timoci Nagusa has 78 from 2010 onward. Julien Arias, shuttling between Colomiers and Stade Français since 2001, stands on 76. Cédric Heymans punched 65 for Toulouse before retiring in 2013, and Castres-Racing-La Rochelle winger Marc Andreu has 60. Julien Candelon rounds out the elite ten with 58 tries for Narbonne and Perpignan during 2003-2012.
